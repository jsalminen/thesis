I think a another factor that is very important is insurance. I'm not a betting woman but I do think that any product with large potential liability insurance cost will get canned no matter how good it might be. In other words if you have an idea for a kitchen chachki or a new gadget for your nail polish this is the place for you. If you have something that requires lots of safety testing (like a new crash helmet design that claims to prevent injury for example) you probably should look up a manufacture of such products to submit your idea to.
