The Description

Current vibrators are pre programmed and one dimensional. They are created using offset weight motors which limit their vibrational output. This uses a technology similar to a speaker such that complex vibrations can be created over a range of frequencies, meaning each individual can find the one that's perfect for them. Link this with an app where programs can be created combining different frequencies, wave functions, power and duration of vibrations and there are infinite possibilities. Programs could also be downloaded which other users may have found particularly 'enjoyable'. The product would be silicone coated for a soft skin compatible touch and waterproof.
