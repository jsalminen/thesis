New Name choosing format

I like the new "This or that" format for the first "go around".

I think Quirky should add another format though as a "2nd round", where we get to choose through the "finalists". A good way to do that would maybe be a "Bracket" format, ie, like the NCAA tournament. Have the Top 16 "finalists" for the name in a bracket, and then you can "fill out" your bracket. Your influence then could be which round you put the ultimate winner through to (the most influence for making it your "winner" as well, least influence for not making it win a round at all, but, still "participating").
