It’s been over two years since the original Cordies were released, and the line has never stopped expanding: from Metal Cordies to the versatile Cordlet, Quirky has a Cordie for almost everyone. However, there’s one frontier we still haven’t crossed, and it’s high time for Cordies, like Dylan and the automobile, to finally go electric. Quirky is proud to introduce Cordies+ this device not only keeps your cables on the tables, but provides four extra USB ports that you can connect to your computer, or plug into a power source for charging. An internal 300g weight ensures that Cordies+ stays exactly where it should be (along with your cables).

Features:
- Cord management
- 4 USB ports for docking and charging
- Weighted base to keep cables in place

Materials:
- Extruded Aluminum shell
- Thermoplastic elastomer core

Dimensions:
- 28mm H x 91mm W x 68mm D

Price, qty and features subject to change during production process.
