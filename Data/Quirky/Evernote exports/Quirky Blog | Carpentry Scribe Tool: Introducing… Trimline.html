Carpentry Scribe Tool: Introducing… Trimline

posted 16 days ago by Nathaniel

Any handyman will tell you: nothing beats having the right tool for the job. That was the thought behind Trimline, a carpenter’s tool that makes it easy to scribe lines at various heights, quickly and accurately. Just insert a pencil, flip the tool to find the proper measurement, and run it along the surface to mark your cut line. Trimline also allows you to mark your 3/16” trim reveals in a snap, taking the guesswork out of the process. It’s every carpenter and weekend warrior’s best friend.

Seeking to make the task of scribing carpentry lines a little less onerous, inventor Jim Curtiss came up with this simple tool that immediately won over the Quirky community. Trimline was influenced by 1,388 contributors and is now ready for it’s Upcoming page debut. Drop by to learn more about it and help influence the final price.

Features:
– 1/4”, 3/8”, 1/2” and 3/4” scribe heights
– 3/16” reveal line marking
– Holds a #2 pencil
– Measurements labeled in inches

Materials:
– Aluminum
– Rubber

Dimensions:
– 51mm L x 19mm W x 29mm D

Price, qty and features subject to change during production process.
