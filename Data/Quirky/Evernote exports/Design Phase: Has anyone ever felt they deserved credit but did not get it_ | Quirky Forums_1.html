It was a really big issue at one time when people saw their unselected ideas used in the product introduced into Upcoming.

But the Quirky design staff (QDS) kinda resolved it when they declared that they aren't selecting the ideas they eventually used in the product, but the ideas that helped move the development of the product along at that moment.
The QDS declared the same thing about CMF (since they often rewarded the voters choosing one color, eventually used the other color, but didn't want to change the % influence from one set of voters to the other.)
