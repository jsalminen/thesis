Ethnographic Research

Over the past few months, we’ve been refining the design for Kate Vallon’s Pod Power, in preparation for its jump to manufacturing. While most of the details have been ironed out thanks to good old-fashioned engineering, the question of cord length endures: Pod Power is driven by the need for more accessible power, but it’s hard to tell what “accessible” means unless you know how the product will be used.

The purpose of this project is to better understand how Pod Power would be used in a real world setting. To this end, we’re asking members to recreate a potential use case, using string and tape as a substitute for the actual product.

Please read over the directions and informative photographs in this blog post: http://www.quirky.com/blog/post/2012/09/help-me-obi-wan-kenobi-youre-my-only-hope/
