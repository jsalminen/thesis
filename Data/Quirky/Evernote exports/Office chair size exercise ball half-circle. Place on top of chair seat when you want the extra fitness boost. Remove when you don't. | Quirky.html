The Problem

They make exercise balls that you sit on instead of an office chair, but that's a big commitment. They also take up room and most offices won't allow you to bring one in when everyone else is sitting in a uniform office chair. But sometimes you want an extra fitness boost while sitting at your desk.

The Solution

Create an exercise ball half-circle that fits on top of a standard office chair seat. Use it as much as you like, whenever you like, and always have the option of sitting in your regular chair seat when you want to instead. No need to bring in a giant exercise ball to replace your regular chair. And you you can easily hide the exercise ball half-circle accessory under your desk when not in use. (Currently the half-circles available on the market are too big for an office chair seat.)

Key Features

  • Creates the option of fitness and good posture while at your desk, but doesn't require a commitment to sitting that way.
  • Easy to store/hide when not in use because it doesn't take up extra space like a full-size ball.
  • Fits any standard office chair seat area.

Product Comparison

  • Full size exercise balls that take up space in the office and require you to give up your regular chair.
