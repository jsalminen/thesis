Project Brief

CMF: Cmf

In this project we’ll be choosing the colors, materials, and finishes for the finalized Rice-for-One product. This product allows you to measure rice and water (for either microwave or stove cooking), rinse easily with a straining top, and is an easy-serving bowl for one.


Please refer to the project timeline for more information.
