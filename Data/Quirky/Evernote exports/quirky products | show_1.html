Features:
-900 Joules of surge protection
-15A cord
-Low profile power adapter with on/off switch
-Two mounting methods – keyholes or adhesive strips
-Sliding clips for cord management
-Safe for laptops, lamps, cell phones, etc.

Materials:
-Reinforced plastic pods
-Rubber coated and insulated wire
-Plastic cord management clips
-Adhesive pads

Dimensions:
-15’ long. 3’ between each pod
