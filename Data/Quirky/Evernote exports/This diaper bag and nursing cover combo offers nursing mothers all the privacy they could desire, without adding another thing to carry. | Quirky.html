This diaper bag and nursing cover combo offers nursing mothers all the privacy they could desire, without adding another thing to carry.

Other
Submitted by: Katherine Buchholz
.

The Description

This patent pending diaper bag quickly and easily converts into a privacy cover for nursing moms. The five roomy pockets can carry all a mom's baby needs for a day out, and everything can be easily accessed at either stage of the conversion, allowing nursing moms to multitask easily. Also, the semi-rigid neckline allows the mom a clear view of her baby without allowing a view to anyone else, keeping this private moment private, even in a crowd. Watch a demo video by going here: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XB2iKnr8i5c

The Customer

Mothers who wish to nurse their children are the target audience for this product. This product would also make the perfect baby shower gift for expecting moms.

Key Features

  • 5 roomy pockets, three of which can be accessed from the top or bottom depending on the use, allow the mom to carry everything she needs for a day or weekend away with the baby.
  • a series of easy snap closures allows for one handed conversion when quarters are tight and hands are in short supply.
  • the semi rigid neckline allows only the mom a clear view of her baby.
  • the adjustable strap can convert this from messenger style bag to hand bag with only the push of a button.

Product Comparison

  • There are tons of nursing covers on the market, but none that do double duty and convert into a usable diaper bag.
