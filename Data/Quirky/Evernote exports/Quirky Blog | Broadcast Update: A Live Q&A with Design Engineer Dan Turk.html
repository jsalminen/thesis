Broadcast Update: A Live Q&A with Design Engineer Dan Turk

posted 2 months ago by baron

Well folks, this is it: the end of the quarter has arrived, and we are less than five days away from our next week-long blackout. There will be no Feedback Friday this week, on account of Friday’s Town Meeting (6pm ET), so we’ll be rounding out the quarter in style with tomorrow’s “Gin with… Dan Turk” (Tuesday, 5pm ET), the latest episode of our staff Q&A series. As a design engineer on the Quirky Design Staff, Dan plays a pivotal role in our collaborative design process, along with the behind-the-scenes work that goes into prepping a product for manufacturing. If you’d like the inside scoop on what it takes to bring inventions from sketch to store, this is the guy to see.

While we’ll have a selection of questions to start, viewers will be able to share questions of their own in the chat, so be sure to stop by the Quirky Live page at 5pm ET tomorrow. For a better idea of what you can expect, check out the recording of our last episode, “Gin with… Jack Lukic”:
