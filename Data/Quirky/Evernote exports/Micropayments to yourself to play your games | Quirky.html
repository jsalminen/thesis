Community Design: Community Design Project

In this project, we want you to submit sketches, images, videos, and other visuals that illustrate industrial design directions for Jason Hunter’s Smart Piggy Bank.

Children like to play console, computer and mobile games but don't like to save money. Parents don't like children playing too much and would like to see them learn to save. Smart Piggy Bank could implement micropayments familiar from mobile games, but here the money would go to the child. The child could buy playtime by putting money to the bank. If he wants to play more, he has to save more.
