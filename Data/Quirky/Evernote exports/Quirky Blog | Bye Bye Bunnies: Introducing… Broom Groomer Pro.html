Bye Bye Bunnies: Introducing… Broom Groomer Pro

posted about 1 month ago by baron

For years, Quirky’s Broom Groomer has fought to keep dust bunnies at bay, but some messes are too big for any one tool to tackle alone. Introducing Broom Groomer Pro, a dustpan/broom combo that serves as a one-stop shop for even the toughest cleanses. Its familiar design still grooms brooms like a champ, but a deeper pan and upright handle help take the hassle out of heavier jobs. Once cleaning is complete, both tools clip together neatly for easy, compact storage. Behold: the first domestic partnership that actually makes life easier.

As the one-time owner of a cleaning service, inventor Anand Goswami knows his way around clean, and he rolled his collective know-how into an invention dubbed Goswami’s 3 in 1 Dust Pan. His no-nonsense design was picked as a perfect fit for Quirky, and with the help of 7956 community influencers, Anand’s invention is now ready to bring a little muscle to the Broom Groomer family. Head to Quirky’s Upcoming section to learn more about the latest member of our cleaning line, and to help us determine its price!

Features:
– Flexible teeth keep your broom clean and neat
– Upright handle makes sweeping effortless
– Broom and dustpan clip together for compact storage
– Rubber handles provide a firm grip

Materials:
– Aluminum
– Polypropylene
– Thermoplastic elastomer

Dimensions:
– Broom: 230mm W x 1010mm L x 60mm D
– Dustpan: 280mm W x 250mm L x 70mm D
– Dustpan Handle: 830mm L

Price, qty and features subject to change during production process.
