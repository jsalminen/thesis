Creative Director's Notes

Notes about the types of submissions we're looking for

We've got to admit, we love this new naming system: in fact, we love it so much that we've decided to come back for seconds!

While hundreds of great names were submitted to the Kitchen Disk naming project, we found that a large number focused on the product's flat form factor (Kitchen Disks), its ability to stack (Flat Stacks), or its general role as a kitchen tool (Kitchen Kit). However, only a few referred to the product's unique foldability, or the compactness that this folding feature allows.

This seemed like a missed opportunity, so to be 100% sure that we picked the best name possible, we've decided to re-open the naming project for another four days. All previously-submitted names will remain to prevent repetition, but all users will get an additional 3 submissions as well. Thus, if you've already submitted three names to the project, don't hesitate to dive in and submit a few more.¬†

Remember: this time around, focus your attention on the product's folding design, along with its compact size. If you can think of a name that references both, all the better! We cant' wait to see what you come up with.¬†

How Naming Works

How do I participate? Compare twenty names side by side and pick your favorites, or submit up to 6 of your own product name submission. As the community participates, we rank names based on popularity. This helps us determine the best names for our products!

How does a name get chosen? The Quirky design staff sifts through the most popular submissions to find a name that perfectly fits the product's design aesthetic.

What happens when a name gets picked? If you submit the name Quirky selects, you will earn 3% influence and receive perpetual royalties on the product. Was your name not chosen? Don't fret; by comparing twenty sets, you still earn a shared piece of influence and will be listed as an influencer on this product!
