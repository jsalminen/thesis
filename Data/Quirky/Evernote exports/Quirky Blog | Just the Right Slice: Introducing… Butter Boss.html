Just the Right Slice: Introducing… Butter Boss

posted 24 days ago by baron

Seriously folks, what would we do without butter? Butter is the stuff that makes everything better, which is why we’re thrilled to unveil Butter Boss, an incredible device that keeps your favorite foodstuff ever close-at-hand, whether you need it sliced, spread, or simply stored. A sliding button makes it easy to measure out perfect golden portions, which can be neatly sliced with a one-handed motion, or spread directly onto food. An easy-clean cap also doubles as a drip tray, making Butter Boss just at home on the dinner table as it is in the kitchen. It’s butter, faster, funner.

With several incredible cooking contraptions already under their belt, invention duo Jenson is well on their way to reinventing the modern kitchen. They continued this trend with their latest concept for a better butter slicer, and with a helping hand from 3062 community influencers, Butter Boss is ready to join Scoop and Silo in Quirky’s portion control portfolio. Head to Quirky’s Upcoming section to learn more about our latest launch, and to help us determine its price!

Features:
– Versatile design can either slice or spread a stick of butter
– Sliding button lets you measure out perfect portions, in either tbsp. or tsp.
– Metal wire and lever action enable one-handed slicing
– Hand-held form factor makes spreading just as easy
– Easy-clean cap doubles as a drip tray for tabletop serving
– Compact design stores vertically or horizontally

Materials:
– Plastic
– Metal

Dimensions:
– 50mm W x 153mm H x 50mm D

Price, qty and features subject to change during production process.
