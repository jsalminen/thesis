Broadcast Update: How to Craft the Perfect Product Name

posted 12 days ago by baron

On last week’s episode of Feedback Friday, the Quirky Platform team gave a breakdown of our new naming system, providing some great insight into how it works, and using data to demonstrate just how much the changes have improved our branding process. If you missed out on the discussion, you can watch a full recording below, but this week, we’d like to direct our attention to the other half of this equation: the names themselves.

That’s right folks, it’s a Naming Feedback Friday. While most product names seem deceptively simple, the process of crafting them is not, and can involve everything from customer psychology to onomatopoeia (i.e. “Swiffer). Since naming agencies have a team of specialists to help them brainstorm, we decided to give our community some backup by hosting a broadcast on the subject: specifically, what we look for in a name, and how to go about crafting the perfect name for a product.

The panel will feature Invention Ambassador Anna Buchbauer, along with Resident Storyteller Stephen Morse, so we’ll be flaunting some major credentials on this one (did you know that Anna has a Masters in Branding?). Whether you’ve been submitting names for years, or are just gearing up for your first, we guarantee you won’t come away empty-handed.

So how can you get involved in this stars-studded symposium? Just head to Quirky.com/live this Friday at 3pm ET, and be ready with your questions! If you can’t make the live discussion, you can post your questions in the comment section below: we’ll handle the rest.
