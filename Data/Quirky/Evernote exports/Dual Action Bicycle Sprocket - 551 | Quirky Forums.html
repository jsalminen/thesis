Dual Action Bicycle Sprocket - 551

This is Andrew, one of Quirky's Invention Ambassadors!
The Quirky Design Staff are aware that a lot of valuable information comes through these forums and Get Satisfaction, but lots of it get's lost in the noise of general discussions. To make sure the right feedback is going to the right people, we'd like to give you spaces to share your thoughts and concerns about products in development.
If you find an interesting article or have done your own research regarding the topic product, feel free to post it in this thread. Any informative comments or specific questions relating to the product are welcome!
We hope this will spur some great discussions about what we are working on at Quirky, and will better connect the forums to the rest of the Quirky process.
Please keep the discussion professional and constructive: we're looking for ideas and feedback, but will remove any comments unrelated to the development of the product.

Here is the direct link to the product's timeline: http://www.quirky.com/products/551/timeline
