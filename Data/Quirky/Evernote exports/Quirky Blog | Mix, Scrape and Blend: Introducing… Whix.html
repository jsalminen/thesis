Mix, Scrape and Blend: Introducing… Whix

posted 3 days ago by baron

Unless you’ve got arms like Mike Tyson, the act of mixing can turn baking from an art into a workout. Give your forearms a break with Whix, a specialized mixing tool that makes it easy to blend ingredients in batters, burgers, and other thick mixtures. Every feature and function was designed with mixing in mind: special tines allow ingredients to pass through for better distribution, and Whix’s curved shape perfectly matches the contour of a bowl. Silicone ribs in the back also help scrape excess batter off the bottom of your bowl. It’s mixing, mastered.

When mixing heavy cookie doughs, inventor Carla Leming would always use a serving fork, but wished its better blending came with the flexibility and scraping power of a spatula as well. To solve the problem, Carla came up with a design that combined those two culinary tools, and with the help of 507 community influencers, Whix will soon be ready to take the “ache” out of baking. Head to Quirky’s Upcoming section to learn more about our latest launch, and to help us determine its price!

Features:
– Specialized design to tackle thick mixtures
– Dual tines for better distribution
– Curved head matches the contour of a bowl
– Silicone ribs to scrape excess ingredients
– Hole in handle for easy hanging

Materials:
– Silicone
– Nylon

Dimensions:
– 310mm L x 73mm W

Price, qty and features subject to change during production process.
