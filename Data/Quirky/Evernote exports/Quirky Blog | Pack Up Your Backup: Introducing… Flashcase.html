Pack Up Your Backup: Introducing… Flashcase

posted 19 days ago by Nathaniel

When it comes to Apple accessories, you should never have to choose between style and functionality. Introducing Flashcase, a MacBook case with built-in storage that doesn’t intrude on the laptop’s sleek and simple design. Its molded shell protects against bumps and scratches, and its pleated pockets provide discreet accessory storage, remaining slim when empty, but expanding to fit even the chunkiest of hard drives. Best of all, a convenient pass-through slot for cables allows a hard drive to be accessed without leaving its pocket. Who knew the first stylish fanny pack would be made for a computer?

Fed up with dangling external harddrives and vulnerable cables, inventor Sean Stewart submitted his idea for a laptop case that accommodates external peripherals to the Quirky community for consideration. It won over the Eval panel, and after a little help from 2,801 community influencers, Flashcase is ready for it’s Upcoming page debut. Head over there today to learn more about the newest Quirky product, and help us find the ideal price for it.

Features:
- Slim cloth pockets provide discreet storage
– Molded shell protects against scratches and bumps
– Pleated pockets expand to accomodate accessories and hard drives
– Pass-through slot allows hard drives to connect from within a pocket

Materials:
– Plastic shell
– Cotton and poly blended fabric
– Nylon zipper

Dimensions:
– 370mm W x 255mm H x 30mm D

Price, qty and features subject to change during production process.
