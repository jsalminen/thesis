Minimalistic alarm clock that integrates into an app that has "smart alarms" that will automatically see your earliest event and set itself

Electronics
Submitted by: Ryan Pendleton
.

The Problem

People forget to set alarms, or have to go through finding a way to set a "backup" alarm for special or important early events. Even when you remember to set your alarm, unless you have a consistent schedule you may have to consult your calendar to see what time you have to wake up the next day. Finally, app enabled alarm clocks can be too spendy or confusing to use.

The Solution

The app allows you to make a "smart alarm" just like you can make a smart playlist in iTunes. Have the app access your calendar in iOS and automatically wake you up X hours before the earliest event. Days off? Perhaps let the smart alarm set the wake up time for just before sunrise, or at a constant time. The app can also set the alarm for a backup with a different tone in case you hit snooze to many times.

Key Features

  • No feature creep, simple and easy to use
  • Slick and minimal design, no need to keep buying a new alarm clock for the new features
  • Smart alarms looks at your daily schedule and sets your wake up time for you
  • Out of the norm early event tomorrow? The app can optionally remind you to go to bed early
  • Application integrates into your calendar on your iPhone, no other alarm clock/app does this.
  • App/Alarm hybrid allows the ability to add great features down to road for no additional cost!
  • No need to ever open the app, your alarm is already set! App can customize Alarm Tone and change Smart Alarm settings
  • Clock sets itself from your iPhone's time, never see a blinking 12:00 AM again
