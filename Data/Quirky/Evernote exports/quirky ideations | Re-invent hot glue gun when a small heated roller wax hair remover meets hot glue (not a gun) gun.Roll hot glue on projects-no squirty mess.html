Re-invent hot glue gun when a small heated roller wax hair remover meets hot glue (not a gun) gun.Roll hot glue on projects-no squirty mess

Electronics
Submitted by: Heather Chillemi

The Problem

Hot glue guns are extremely useful, however, they are also a pain, always leaking, falling over when not in use, burning yourself on the tip, and you can never quite guess how much glue is going to come out or if you put the glue stick in right. Below are some examples of the roller was hair removal products (they stand upright), and you can see how easy it would be to turn it into a new style of hot glue gun. The rolling glue gun can give a more precise controlled amount of glue, wether you need a lot (roll many times) or just a little.

The Solution

Turn the awkward glue gun into a glue gun with an easy, ergonomically designed rolling glue dispencer. This "roller" gun can have interchangeable heads for large or small projects, but it would be much easier to use then the gun...there is a dial controlling the amount of glue you would like to dipence...1 for a little to 5 for more glue to be pushed out of roller.   Two rollers are used on this applicator.   They are  separated  by a small gap and that gap controls the application amount.   One roller applies the glue while the other roller the thickness of the glue on the application roller.   This is the same kind of design used in industrial roller coaters.        http://www.beautyexpress.co.uk/product-Hive-Hand-Held-Roller-Depi-Heater-PW100.htm

http://www.satinsmooth.com/product_info.php?pcID=1_90_100&products_id=407

Key Features

  • Rolls glue on whatever you are working on, great for crafting and getting glue in tiny areas
  • Interchangeable heads for tiny projects or large projects that need a lot of glue
