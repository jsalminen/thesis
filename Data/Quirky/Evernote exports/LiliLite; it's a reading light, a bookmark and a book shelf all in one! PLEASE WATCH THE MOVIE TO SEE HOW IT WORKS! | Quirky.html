The Description

LiliLite is an ingenious reading light that you can hang above your bed. It's a reading light, a bookmark and a book shelf all in one. Please take a look at http://vimeo.com/31842039 to see how it works. SUGGESTION: Wire hanging out looks bad. Get this light solar powered or battery powered.
