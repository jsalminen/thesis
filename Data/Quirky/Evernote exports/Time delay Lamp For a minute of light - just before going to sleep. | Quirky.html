The Description

Some times we need light for just a couple of minutes, especially before going to sleep.
Most of us don't have the light switches by the bed or even a night lamp.
Even if you have a night lamp you need to turn it on then go back to the room's switch and turn it off, then come back to bed and turn off the bed lamp.
All of that for a minute of light.

The Customer

Anyone who needs a minute or two of light before going to sleep.

Key Features

  • Easily Plugged to a any socket - by the bed or at the room entrance.
  • Just tap the lamp to turn it on - it will turn off by itself.
