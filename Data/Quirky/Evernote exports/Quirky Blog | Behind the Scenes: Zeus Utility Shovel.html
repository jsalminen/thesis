Behind the Scenes: Zeus Utility Shovel

posted 25 days ago by baron

Summer is fast approaching, but a wise inventor always thinks ahead, which is why we’re doing some hardcore testing to get the Zeus utility shovel ready for production. Thanks to our snazzy new Fortus 400mc 3D printer, the design staff was able to craft a prototype out of bona fide polycarbonate (the final production material), but with temperatures rising well into the 50′s, finding a suitable testing ground proved difficult at best.

In the end, designer Aaron Saxton and design engineer Laura Sink had to get creative, and headed to the Chelsea Piers skating rink to find the last little snowdrift in NYC. This is the story of what happened next…
