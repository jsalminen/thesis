An Interview with Twelve-Time Inventor Marc Zech

posted 11 days ago by baron

You know him, you love him, and if you don’t, you probably know his inventions. His name is Marc Zech and he’s dreamed up more Quirky products than any other inventor, from the classic Scratch-n-Scroll to the ultra-versatile Bandits. We couldn’t verify this, but we’re pretty sure his brain is made of solid gold.

However, while Marc and his inventions have left an indelible mark on Quirky, Ben Kaufman frequently points out that we’ve never actually met the guy. That’s why last week, we flew Marc all the way from Hamburg, Germany for a visit to QHQ, where we got the chance to meet the brain behind some of our most iconic products.
