Community Refine Project


After researching available foldable bike helmets, non-folding options, and looking over community concept submissions, the Quirky Design Staff decided that the most efficient way to make a helmet smaller was to simply cut it in half! 
  • The top half of the helmet nests in the bottom half while staying connected by a hinge on the back of the product.  
  • The helmet folds down into a convenient shape that can be thrown into a bag with the rest of your belongings. 

We’ve included two possible directions for this two part helmet: 
  1. One option is sealed by a zipper with fabric covering a hard plastic shell with foam on the interior. 
  2. The second option utilizes a latch system similar to a snowboard binding, these latches could possibly be reflective. This option would have a plastic exterior with a foam interior.
Please vote on the concept you like the best. Also, if you have any comments about either of the designs, be sure to share!
