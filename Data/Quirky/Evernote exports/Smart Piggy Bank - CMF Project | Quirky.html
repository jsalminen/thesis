Cmf Project

We’re almost at the end of the product development round for the Smart Piggy Bank, but before we launch, we need your help choosing some colors.

In this project, we want you to vote on the fun / pattern color finish for this product. We are going to launch the pig in four different finishes to show the possibilites of color and finish. The standard colors will be matte black, matte white, and chrome.

Vote for the version you like the best, and make sure you leave comments and suggestions as we might end up with a version that deviates from one or more of these concepts.
