Broadcast Update: A Live Interview with Designer Julia Troy

posted 13 days ago by baron

Have you ever wished you could dive into the mind of a Quirky designer, getting an inside look at what they’re thinking, how they brainstorm, and how they tackle a bewildering design problem? Well, we’re working on a product for that, but until it’s released we’ve got “Gin with… Julia Troy”, a live interview and Q&A with Quirky’s sunniest senior designer. It’s going down this Tuesday at 5pm ET, and it’s only on Quirky Live.

As the lead designer for some of Quirky’s most innovative products, from the spiral-slicing Cyclone to the Kepler Space Kit, Julia is a master at turning a puzzling problem into a stunning product solution. Over the course of our interview, we’re going to focus our attention on this particular strength, unraveling the process behind some of her most challenging projects and innovative solutions. Viewers will get the chance to pose their own questions in the chat, so whether you’re a veteran Quirky inventor, or a first-timer looking to earn some stripes, we highly recommend tuning in. We guarantee you won’t come away empty handed.

Until then, feel free to pose your questions in the comments below, and check out the recording of our last “Gin with…” broadcast if you’d like a better idea of what you can expect.
