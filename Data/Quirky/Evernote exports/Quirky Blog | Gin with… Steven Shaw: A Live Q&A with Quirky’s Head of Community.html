Gin with… Steven Shaw: A Live Q&A with Quirky’s Head of Community

posted 27 days ago by baron

There’s no denying it: April has been a month of change here at QHQ. Between a company-wide rebranding, a new partnership with GE, and a shiny new naming platform, a lot of serious hustle has taken place, and a community member sitting in the middle of it all might be tempted to ask: “Hey, where do I fit in?”.

Well, get ready for one hell of an answer, because the next episode of our “Gin with…” broadcast was tailor-made for you. It’s “Gin with… Steven Shaw”, a live interview and Q&A with Quirky’s Head of Community, going down this Tuesday at 5pm EDT. While Steven’s own background and experience could fill a book (he’s written two), the conversation will focus on the current state of Quirky, and how projects like the rebranding and upcoming site features will impact the community over the course of 2013. If you have a question about Quirky Pro, the future of the Community Design Phase, or our plans to outreach to specialized communities, then this is the broadcast for you. Just head to the Quirky Live page at 5pm EDT this Tuesday, and be ready with your questions!

Alternatively, if you can’t make the broadcast, feel free to post your questions in the comments below, and we’ll share them when the broadcast goes live. Until then, keep inventing, and check out this past episode of “Gin with…” if you’d like a better idea of what to expect.
