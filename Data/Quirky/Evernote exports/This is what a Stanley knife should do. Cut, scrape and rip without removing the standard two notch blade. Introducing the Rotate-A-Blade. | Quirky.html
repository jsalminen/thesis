This is what a Stanley knife should do. Cut, scrape and rip without removing the standard two notch blade. Introducing the Rotate-A-Blade.

Other
Submitted by: David Tyers
.

The Description

The Rotate-A-Blade utility knife uses the standard two notch "Stanley" blade. This blade can be safely rotated into different working positions, cutting, scraping and ripping. The user no longer has to purchase special hook blades or flimsy razor blades for a scraper. The working blade can be retracted into the handle as normal. Blade changes are tool-less, great for when a blade is worn out. The handle includes blade storage. This is a link to a youtube video showing one of the first prototypes in action, it gets very interesting at 2 minutes in! http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v2ZH9eELbRY
Should be able to adjust the lenght of the blade that sticks out.

The Customer

Home owners, painters, fabricators, warehouse workers, construction workers, and anyone that uses a utility knife or scraper

Key Features

  • Rotating standard two notch blade
  • Rotating blade into different positions
