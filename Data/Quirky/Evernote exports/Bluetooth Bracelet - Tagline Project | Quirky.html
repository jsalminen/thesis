Tagline Project

Hey everyone, this is Stephen, Quirky’s Resident Storyteller. We need your help coming up with a better tagline for our Bluetooth Bracelet. Remember, every time you nail it, you get fame, fortune, and lots of high fives. Here’s what to think about when you’re thinking up names and taglines:

The Bluetooth Bracelet Brief: It’s a fashionable bracelet for on-the-go and stylish women that, in typical Quirky form, doubles as a stealthBluetooth headset, so you aren’t slowed down by rummaging through your bag to find your phone every time it rings. When you hear the ring, the bracelet simultaneously vibrates and shines a subtle light. You easily unlock the spring-loaded hinge to automatically answer the call. (We call this Flip Phone 3.0 technology!) The bracelet’s design is minimalist, sleek, and trendy.
