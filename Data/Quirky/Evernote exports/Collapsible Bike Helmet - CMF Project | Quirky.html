We’re almost at the end of the product development for Michael Yoch’s Collapsible Bike Helmet. Before we launch, we need your help deciding what color scheme would work the best.

Based on the feedback we have gathered from the community during the Refine Project we feel that the zipper option offers the invention the most chance for success.

After prototyping the Zipper Option, the Quirky Design Staff thought that it was be a much better user experience than the Latch Option. They liked how the user can quickly unzip the helmet and put it into their bag without much effort. Whereas the latch option caused the user might struggle more with configuring the helmet in the position that they preferred. Although there was some concern about hair getting caught inside the zipper,  many trails of the prototype confirmed this was not an issue because the user is zipping or unzipping when the product is off of their head.
