Community Design

In this project we want you to submit sketches, images, videos, and other visuals that illustrate design directions for Katherine Buchholz’s Nursing Diaper Bag. We will take the top ideas as inspiration towards the final design.


Designer Tip: Before submitting, please keep in mind that this is a two-part product; a nursing blanket integrated with a diaper bag. We’d love it if you submitted ideas that included these two essential elements.
