How Naming Works

How do I participate? Compare twenty names side by side and pick your favorites, or submit up to 3 of your own product name submission. As the community participates, we rank names based on popularity. This helps us determine the best names for our products!

How does a name get chosen? The Quirky design staff sifts through the most popular submissions to find a name that perfectly fits the product's design aesthetic.

What happens when a name gets picked? If you submit the name Quirky selects, you will earn 3% influence and receive perpetual royalties on the product. Was your name not chosen? Don't fret; by comparing twenty sets, you still earn a shared piece of influence and will be listed as an influencer on this product!
