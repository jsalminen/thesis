  • What is Quirky Pro?
  • Quirky Pro is an annual membership program that gives you unlimited idea submissions, the ability to put any one of your ideas automatically into “under consideration” and also a Quirky Pro Kit filled w/ Quirky swag.
  • How much does it cost to become a Quirky Pro?
  • A yearly membership is normally $199, however during this introductory period, you can become a Quirky Pro for just $99!
  • How much does an idea submission normally cost?
  • It normally costs $10 to submit an idea to Quirky. With Quirky Pro, you can submit as many ideas as you like for an entire year. Wow!
  • What does it mean to put an idea into “under consideration”?
  • Quirky gets hundreds to thousands of ideas a week and when the community helps us surface the best ones, we move them into “under consideration”. Each week, every idea in this bucket gets carefully reviewed by the Quirky Design Staff (QDS) to decide who will be crowned the next Quirky inventor and have their idea brought to life. Even for ideas that are not selected, the inventor will still receive detailed feedback from QDS on what they thought, in addition to suggestions on how to improve the idea if they have any. This Quirky Pro benefit allows you to put any one of your ideas into “under consideration” at any time. But be careful, as you only get one of these per year.
  • When can I put my idea into “under consideration”?
  • You may put your idea into “under consideration” anytime after it’s been live for 48 hours. The reason is to give the community a chance to vote, collaborate and comment on your idea!
