I would like to have mini cameras inside my fridge so that I can view the contents from a smart phone while I'm at the grocery store.

Kitchen
Submitted by: David Lent
.

The Problem

I would like to see if it is possible to build a universal unit that would install in almost any fridge. The biggest problem would be how to supply power to the cameras and wifi unit with out drilling holes into fridge. I'm sure a battery unit can be made, but how do you connect the unit to the fridge to operate the light bulb with out drilling holes for wires? Another issue to tackle would be to find cameras that can handle long term exposure to cold temps. Or, sell the idea to a fridge manufacturer and have it built in.

The Solution

I suppose a by pass switch could be connected to the mechanical switch that turns the light on when the door is open. Perhaps one small hole may need to be drilled near the switch?

Key Features

  • Convienient, easy to use, saves me money from buying items I alredy have
