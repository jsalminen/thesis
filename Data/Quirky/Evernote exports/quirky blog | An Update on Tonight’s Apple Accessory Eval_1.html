An Update on Tonight’s Apple Accessory Eval

posted about 11 hours ago by baron

Due to the special nature of tonight’s Apple Accessories Eval, the process is going to be handled a little differently from that of our regular Thursday evaluations. Here are some of the changes you can expect:

- Ideas from the Apple Accessories vertical will not be marked Under Consideration: instead, staff members are using an internal system to evaluate submissions, which will not be reflected on the public site. Thus, if you see that your ideation has not been moved to UC by 7pm, don’t worry: it is absolutely still eligible for Eval.

- We’re also increasing the number of idea submissions that will be discussed: rather than the usual 10-13, we’ll be doing a rapid-fire analysis of several dozen submissions. We hope you’ve had your Red Bull.

- Due to the fast pace of this project, inventors will not be contacted by the Invention Ambassadors prior to Eval. As a result, the selection of products discussed at Eval will be a surprise to both the inventors and the majority of staff. Members are encouraged to tune in via the Quirky Live page at 7pm ET to watch the Eval in real time: if you have any important points or embellishments to share during the course of the discussion, mention it in the live chat, and we’ll make sure that the info is passed along.

- This accelerated pace also means that influence will be handled more fluidly: it will be difficult to identify similar submissions on the fly, so the majority of influence for selected submissions will be awarded at a later date. This does not mean that the influence will be handled ad hoc: careful records will be kept of the proceedings, and a team of staff members has been chosen to keep track of all design activity, and ensure that the proper influence is awarded to contributing members.

Thanks for listening folks, we hope you’re as excited for tonight as we are! If you have any questions or concerns about the workshop over the course of the night, feel free to give us a call at 212-389-1390: we’ll have support reps on call to get you squared away.
