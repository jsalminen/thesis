One Bake, Two Cakes: Introducing… Layer Up

posted 17 days ago by Nathaniel

Maybe it’s just us, but baking a cake should never be a stressful experience. Nevertheless, bakers still go to hair-pulling lengths to ensure their layered cakes are made just right. That’s why we designed Layer Up, a specialized pan that bakes two identical 8” cakes, producing easy, uniform layers each and every time. A center channel distributes the batter equally between both basins, ensuring that both layers bake at the same rate. With silicone grips for comfort, and a non-stick surface for easy cleaning, Layer Up makes layered cakes as fun to bake as they are to eat.

Inventor Jackie Siddall wanted an easy, error free way to evenly distribute batter for her layered cakes into two pans. She submitted her idea for a double basin cake pan to the Quirky community and in no time it was picked up for development. 1,258 influencers contributed to Layer Up, helping make it into the product you can see on Quirky’s Upcoming page today. Head on over to learn more about it and to influence the price of the final product.

Features:
– Dual 8” basins for a perfect layered cake every time
– Center channel distributes batter for uniform height and bake time
– Heat-resistant silicone handles
– Nonstick baking surface

Materials:
– Metal
– Silicone

Dimensions:
-Pan (x2): 203mm Diameter x 51mm Deep
-Frame: 483mm L x 241mm W

Price, qty and features subject to change during production process.
