A mailbox app. Because not all mail comes through an inbox.

Submitted by: Sarah Streitwieser
.

The Problem

You don't know when the mail gets put in your mailbox. You're phone currently lets you know when you have an email, but not when you have real mail.

The Solution

A small device placed in the mailbox that is motion activated. When it is triggered, it will send a message to your phone to let you know to check the mail (the stuff in the mailbox). This is especially helpful in bad weather, when you're waiting on the mail but don't want to go outside. It is also nice to be alerted when you have mail if you are someone who does not get mail or whose mailbox is not physically close to them (PO Boxes or apartment-style living).

  • Checks your mailbox for you
