Submitted by: MarcR

The Problem

Anyone that lives in a building with a communal laundry room knows that it's always a pain to run to and from the laundry room to check if your washer or dryer is finished. This can include college dorms and apartment buildings both on campus and in cities. Homeowners who are busy running around doing housework don't have time to keep checking if the wash is done as well, especially if the laundry room is located in a basement. Now you can know when your wash is done.

Anyone whose ever had a car, bike, computer bag, purse or luggage stolen, had your child walk away while you weren't looking or lost a dog knows the panic, fear and frustration that sets in. Now you can track anything.

Ever lose your keys, purse or backpack? Now you can find them.

Want to make sure your suitcase isn't tampered with in your hotel room? How about if you need to leave something and want to make sure it's not moved or disturbed. Now you can know if your items are secure.

The Solution

The Multi-Function Key Fob has many features:

LAUNDRY ALERT MODE: Hardware: A small key fob with detachable magnetic bracket that can attach to the side of the washer/dryer or clip to a laundry basket that is set on top of the washer/dryer. When you start the wash or drying cycle, simply activate your iPhone app. One minute after the washing or drying cycle stops, The vibration sensor will note that the machine has stopped and Laundry Alert will send out a message to your iPhone alerting you that the wash is done.

Each Laundry Alert key fob pairs exclusively with your iPhone to help deter theft or it can be tracked thru it's GPS function. Also, it can be magnetically attached behind the washer or dryer so it is not visible. When you are finished, simply retrieve the fob. Another option would be to attach the key fob to or set it inside of a laundry basket which you set on top of the washer/dryer. The fob can be discretely hidden by covering it with a piece of clothing.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
OK, I recognize that the following functions exist on other individual products currently on the market. However, they don't all exist in one unit nor do they come with an app to control them from your phone. The Laundry Alert is the main feature of my idea but since the device has to be built, they might as well add in as much functionality as possible even if there are similar stand alone products currently on the market.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
TRACK ANYTHING: Clip it to your child’s backpack or put in a coat pocket. Visiting the mall and your child goes missing. Call up the GPS portion of the app and track your child’s position. Leave it in your car. Car stolen? Track it. Clip it to your bike, your dog’s collar, put inside your computer bag or suitcase. Anything that can be stolen or lost can be tracked.

FIND ANYTHING: Clip it to your keychain. The next time your keys go missing, use the app to activate the audible alarm. If the keys are within the range of your iPhone you will hear the alarm and be able to locate them. Not in range? Use the GPS portion to track them.

MOTION SENSOR MODE: Place in your suitcase or computer bag when staying in a hotel. If someone opens your suitcase while you are out, you will get a message that the bag has moved. Hook it on the back of the door. If the door opens while you're out, you'll be alerted. Place the fob within anything you need to set down but don’t want disturbed.

Key Features

  • Convenience
  • Multi-functions

Product Comparison

  • DryerBro iPhone App - The Problem with this app is that you have to leave your iPhone on the washer/dryer in a public space. Too easy to be stolen. Also, the text message is sent to your computer or a friends iPhone. This is hardly convenient if your friends are not around when you are doing laundry plus you use the use of your iPhone while it sits in the laundry room.
  • Zoombak GPS, Garmin and several other small GPS trackers. Zoombak uses an iPhone app others don't
  • Cobra Tags - works with Droids
