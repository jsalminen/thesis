One common complaint about working atop a ladder, is the inevitable inconvenience of climbing down to unplug your power tools from the extension cord on the floor. There is no need to fret though, we’ve got you covered with Prop Power, an extension cord that brings power right where you need it. With flexible wiring housed within conforming plastic and a triple outlet head, Prop Power makes it so you never have to bend and search for a place to plug in your devices again.

Features:
  - 6ft extension cord
  - 3 outlets
  - 18 inches of flex at the outlet end

Materials:
  - Plastic
  - Conductive wiring

Dimensions:
  - Total length: 6ft
  - Outlet end: 93mm H x 30mm W x 22mm D

  

Price, qty and features subject to change during production process.
