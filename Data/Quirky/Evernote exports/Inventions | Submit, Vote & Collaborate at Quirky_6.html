    The Description

    The Soap-Hook is a simple plastic screw hook that is designed to screw into bar soap. This hook is used to hang the bar soap from the shower caddie or shower curtain rod. By hanging the bar soap it is allowed to dry and doesn't get all slimy. When the bar soap is used up the Soap-Hook can be reused for a new bar.

    The Customer

    Anyone that uses bar soap especially in a shower or bath tub.
