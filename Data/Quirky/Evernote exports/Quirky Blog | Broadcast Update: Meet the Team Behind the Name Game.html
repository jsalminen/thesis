Broadcast Update: Meet the Team Behind the Name Game

posted 19 days ago by baron

If you’ve participated in a Branding project recently, you’ve probably noticed our new “this or that” approach to choosing product names, which we unveiled for the first time earlier this month. The Quirky Platform team designed this system to grow and evolve with our community, making the experience more engaging for users, and the data more useful for the team back at QHQ. Two weeks after its launch, the system has been implemented on a number of projects, and it has already produced its first final product name, Butter Boss. So, now that the initial test run is complete, many of you must be wondering: does it work?

Well, we could tell you in writing, but we’d rather you hear it from the team that designed the whole system in the first place. To that end, we’re hosting a special platform-focused episode of Feedback Friday, this Friday at 3pm ET, featuring a live discussion and Q&A with digital product designer Jim England, who played a primary role in designing and building the new naming system.

Over the course of the broadcast, Jim will share his reactions to the first two weeks of naming, breaking down the data to determine just how much the new system has impacted our process. Online viewers will be able to share their questions in the chat as well, so be sure to come prepared. Since Jim will have a hand in almost every web update this year, there’s no better person to ask about the future of Quirky’s community platform.

So, how can you get involved in this special symposium? Just head to Quirky.com/live this Friday at 3pm ET, and be ready with your questions! If you can’t make the live broadcast, just post your questions in the section below, and we’ll share them once we’re on the air (we’ll post a full recording of the broadcast here on the blog).
