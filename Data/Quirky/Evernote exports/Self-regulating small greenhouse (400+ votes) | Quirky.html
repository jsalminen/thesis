Self-regulating small greenhouse (400+ votes)

Garden
Submitted by: DianaW
.

The Problem

Small greenhouses can be manually open and closed, but what if you're not around and it needs to be opened (or closed) ?

The Solution

Add a few small solar panels on top of the glass, put a small device inside that will run on the energy of the solar panels and the device will 'tell' the greenhouse when to open or close the windows. It can measure the warmth/heat and humidity etc. Now your vegetables will grow faster than they ever have !

Another idea is make the device run with an app which will tell you about the warmth/humitidy etc., so you can still control the greenhouse manually, but also from a distance.

I know Quirky is not into solar panel solutions, but this greenhouse will be out in the sun all day and the small device doesn't need much power to work.

If Quirky really dislikes the solar panel, it can also be executed with an already existing product; an automatic window-opener which cooperates with a cylinder that is filled with a kind of paraffin. When it gets hot the window is automatically opened and closed as it cools, without electricity. It will be harder to make it app enabled, so I prefer the solar panel.

Please let me know if you have any suggestions to improve my idea.

If we make a little more expensive version, we can add the following options:

Suggestion by Ken Y : You know how Quirky likes to make things high-tech! I suggest this could have roof panels made of material that could get less transparent as the heat rises and direct sun hits them, thus shading the plants from direct sun. A little "smart" greenhouse!

Suggestion by Naomi : Make it sensor enabled to detect intruding animals.Have it automatically close when they are detected and send you a message of the status. Deluxe model could have a camera to record the type of animal that is after your plants.

Suggestions by Jeroen : -add fresnellenses in(stead of) the windows for optimal light (not to bright) -rotate the greenhouse slowly towards the sun for optimal light -motion sensor to detect birds or other animal -alarm sound to scare birds -open if bird is trapped.

Suggestion by Richa : Maybe have a motion detector to detect flying pests like flies, bees etc, and close based on that too, or auto- spray repellant.

Key Features

  • Self-regulating
  • If app controlled it can be controlled from a distance
  • Better growing vegetables!
