Hey Cole. Congrats on your your idea going into UC! It will now go through our pre-evaluation process and hopefully make it to Eval. The link in the last sentence takes you to a blog post explaining pre-evaluation, but here's a synopsis:

- Your Invention Ambassador, Katherine, will be sending you a list of questions to help us better understand you idea and the inspiration it came from.
- We review each idea that makes it into UC twice. If your idea doesn't make it into Eval the first week, it will have a second chance the following week.
- Katherine will contact you by phone to notify you if your submissions is going to Eval or not on Thursday.

Key take aways are this:
- UC does not guarantee your submission will make it to Eval - it must still go through pre-evaluation
- Even if it doesn't make it the first week, you submission may still go to Eval the following week.

If you have any other questions, please email questions@quirky.com or me directly and the community team will be happy to help out.
