4 IN 1 RECHARGEABLE FLASH-LAMP-LIGHT w/ a push in / stick/ hang up multi lights system. light every where, when you need it.

Other
Submitted by: Julio C Del Castillo
.

The Description

a flash light will multiple functions...each light is attached and will be use as a lamp when light is needed the most. each flash light can be a push in light, stick to the wall or just hang up use system.
Covering large areas when each part is use alone in different spots, plus still use the one in your hand will give you confidence in an emergency situation like today in NY.
Can also be use when fixing your car, when get stuck on the road at night, many different scenarios.

Please check the drawings!!!

Mr Gary G, suggested that this item should have the following features incorporated to make it more interesting:

Magnets
Lanyard loop
Belt clip
Waterproof
Battery sharing electrical connection
Incorporate a Fresnel lens, for fire starting
Mini storage compartment, if room permits
Small solar panel for passive charging
Mold useful features into the housing: ruler/straight edge, etc
Laser pointer with the light housing exterior having perpendicular magnetic connections, , could be used to square things up.

The Customer

Every house should have one.

Key Features

  • 4 in 1 flash light.
  • covers large areas.
  • Anti-slip grips.
