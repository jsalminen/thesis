The Description

Metal realm with rubber tires.Split-board design with rubber support and Big wheels.
Braking system: Light weight metal tubes inserted with heavy duty string, connected from the brake paddle to replaceable brake pads on the wheels for steep descend. The brakes are activated by the movement of the brake paddle in beaded in the middle of the skate board. The brake paddle is secured to the skateboard from the mid section, which allow both ends of the paddle to move up and down.
The brakes paddle works by stepping down on the front or the back end of the paddle with the front tip or back hill of a shoe.
Brake pads are secured with a pivot screw that allow the brake pad to pull forward and squeeze the rotating axle when the brake paddle is activated.
Quirky influence incorporations:
This also has a super adhesive rubber and silicon layer on the top, for full support, and 360 degree turning axles. ShelleyS Suggests- The deck be made of aluminum, which would make it very light weight. For off road use, could come with 'All Terrain' rubber wheels that can be easily changed when needed. This type of board would appeal more to longboard riders, so should be made wider and much longer than the average 32 inch skateboard.
