Eject Power from Pivot Power, Chargeable Outlet/USB where you need it when you need it cell phone charging/power outages. Commuters Outlet

The Problem

Adding a 110 Volt Outlet and USB charger to the Pivot Power will offer consumers the option of removing and transporting charged power when commuting, traveling or in the event of power failure, removable outlet should be designed as LED flashlight (special feature).

The Solution

Outlet would adapt and pivot same as traditional Pivot Power, will eject from Power Strip fully charged and ready to power or charge your portable devices(size and shape ought to be designed in the same manner. With Optional Retractable Cord on demand button:Just press the retractable button and the cord will retracts back into the housing.Neat and compact for commute.
