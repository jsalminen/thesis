The Problem

1) When attaching clipped documents to message board, we quickly run out of space. With Dual Clip we can manage the pace with dual clips 1 clip twice the space.
2) When grouped documents are attached to a clip (to access needed group of doc's),
we have to remove clip from message board
remove all documents,
thumb through docs,
pull the needed doc's,
re- stack (neatly) then re-hang clip
With Dual Clip, we can quickly view the color coded clip that has the document we're needing, simply press the chosen clip and remove needed document with-out removing and re-stacking the entire group of documents.

The Solution

Dual clip is basically a clip inside of a clip or 2 layered clips that function as 1, creating space and ease of accessing & navigating grouped documents,
OPTIONS ON DESIGN:
Can be magnetically mounted,
suction mounted,
cork mounted
designed could harness 2 to 4 clips

Key Features

  • Space Saving
  • Ease of Navigating grouped documents
