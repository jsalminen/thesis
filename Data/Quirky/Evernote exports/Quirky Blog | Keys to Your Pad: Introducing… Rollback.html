Keys to Your Pad: Introducing… Rollback

posted 26 days ago by baron

The iPad is an incredibly versatile machine, but for the avid wordsmith, its simple form leaves something to be desired. Bring back the experience of writing with Rollback, a stylish iPad sleeve that doubles as a writing stand, complete with built-in keyboard. On the go, Rollback’s felt sleeve keeps both iPad and keyboard under cover, safe from dirt and debris. However, when the iPad is removed, the cover rolls back to form an instant stand, transforming your iPad into a 21st-century typewriter. Apple aligned, author approved.

When Marc Zech submitted his original concept for Rollback, he asked the question: “if your iPad needs a sleeve, and it needs a keyboard, why not give it both?”. His call to action caught the community’s attention, and with the help of 2344 influencers, Rollback is now ready to revolutionize the way you write. Head to Quirky’s Upcoming section to learn more about our latest launch, and to help us determine its price!

Features:
– Integrated bluetooth keyboard for typing on the go
– Protective front cover doubles as a stand
– Magnetic clasps for easy transition between storage and stand modes
– USB connection for keyboard charging

Materials:
– Felt body
– Leather details

Dimensions:
– 267mm W x 230mm L x 11mm H

Price, qty and features subject to change during production process.
