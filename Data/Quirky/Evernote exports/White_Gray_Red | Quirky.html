Project Brief

CMF: Cmf Project

We’re almost at the end of the product development round for the 3-in-1 Gift Wrapping Tool, but before we launch, we need your help choosing some colors.

In this project, we want you to vote on the final colors for this product. This tool cuts, tapes, and curls ribbons to take the pain out of wrapping gifts.

Vote for the versions you like the best, and make sure you leave comments and suggestions as we might end up with a version that deviates from one or more of these concepts.
