Glow with the Flow: Introducing… Airlight

posted 23 days ago by baron

Since time immemorial, the thought of installing a ceiling fan has daunted even the handiest of homeowners. That is, until today. Introducing Airlight, a lighted ceiling fan that screws directly into a light socket, creating the first ceiling fan you can actually place yourself. The fan is both quiet and lightweight, and its integrated light bulb means that you’ll never sacrifice illumination in the name of better air flow. With a remote to control dimming and fan speed, and a coated metal finish for timeless style, Airlight will show you just how versatile a ceiling fan can be.

Frustrated with cluttered desks and poor ventilation, inventor Chad Maxey came to Quirky with an idea for a more unobtrusive ceiling fan. His spotlight fan design swayed the Eval crowd with its innovative and efficient approach, and after a little tweaking from 18,659 community influencers, Airlight is now ready to bring quality airflow to college students and handy-capped homeowners alike. Head to Quirky’s Upcoming section to learn more about our latest product launch, and to help us determine its price.

Features:
– Dual function light and ceiling fan in one simple package
– Light socket compatibility for effortless installation
– Variable fan speeds for maximum comfort
– Remote controls light intensity and fan speed

Materials:
– Powder-coated metal
– Plastic

Dimensions:
– Fan: 410mm W x 300mm D
– Remote: 45mm W x 75mm H

Price, qty and features subject to change during production process.
