IPhone 5 APP enabled Holiday lights. Customize Holiday lites with your favorite Holiday theme or Holiday greeting. Now with NeverWet.

Apple_accessories
Submitted by: Wilfredo Martinez
.

The Problem

People want to personalize their Holiday lights.

The Solution

Allowing people to add personalized messages and change Holiday theme will give them a unique opportunity to customize their Holiday lights.

Key Features

  • Bluetooth enabled universal set of LED lights that anyone can program to suit their favorite Hoilday.
  • Choose from your favorire Holiday themes such as Independence Day, Halloween, Thanksgiving, Christmas, Chanukah, New years Day and Kwanzaa.
  • Now protect your outdoor Holiday lights year long with NeverWet.

Product Comparison

  • Custom standard Holiday lights.
