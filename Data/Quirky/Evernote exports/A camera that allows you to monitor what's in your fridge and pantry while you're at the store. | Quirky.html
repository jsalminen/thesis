A camera that allows you to monitor what's in your fridge and pantry while you're at the store.

Wink
Submitted by: Anna McCarty
.

The Problem

When you're living with other people (especially kids), it's a difficult task to keep track of what gets eaten or used up. It would require constantly updating a catalog. (Which I tried to do once and quickly failed to keep up with it.)

I can't count how many times I have called my husband (crossing my fingers and hoping he's at home) to see if he could check on how much milk is left in the fridge or if we have another tube of toothpaste in the cabinet. Just so I can stop at the store on my way back from work without going home and taking inventory of everything first.

What happens if he doesn't answer? I go to the store and either forgo buying something we needed or buy extra of something that we didn't need. Alternatively, I go straight home with the intention to take inventory of what we need and instead I sit down, play with my cat, remember that show I wanted to watch, watch the show, call my friend to tell her how awesome the show was, say hi to my husband who just walked in the door, and then give up on having a healthy dinner for the night because I no longer feel like going to the store... I'll just go tomorrow instead (yeah, right)

The Device

The device would have one or, if needed, multiple wide angle cameras that can take snapshots of your entire fridge, freezer, pantry, laundry room, bathroom cabinets, where ever you store your sundries. (Perhaps it could be purchased one camera at a time for smaller or larger spaces) It could either use a flash or OLED lights to illuminate the space for the photo.

App Features

  • When you open your app you can choose to view what your device sees for you.
  • Additionally you can use it to compile different kinds of shopping lists.
  • Eventually it would either learn your favorites, or you can set favorite items for quick listing.
  • Lastly, you can sync the shopping lists to other devices using the same app, so I can make up a list for my husband if he is able to make it to the store before I am.
