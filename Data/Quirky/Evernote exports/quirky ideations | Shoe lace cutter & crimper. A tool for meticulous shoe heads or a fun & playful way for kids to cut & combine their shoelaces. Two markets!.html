Shoe lace cutter & crimper. A tool for meticulous shoe heads or a fun & playful way for kids to cut & combine their shoelaces. Two markets!

Submitted by: youcancallmephil

The Description

So to get to the point I’m pretty particular about the length of my shoelaces when it comes to tying my shoes. Too long and the tied loops are overly huge; to short and there isn’t just enough loop (or as you called them when you were a kid; bunny ears). This idea goes out to all the shoe heads in the world and those who are meticulous about their style. So here it goes. The problem with some shoes I buy is that the laces come in a length that are just to long, and often they come with laces that you just can’t buy off the shelf. The right laces for the right shoes it seems to be. Off the shelf crimping tools and methods are way to technical and require pliers, glue and sometimes a hammer in order to complete the job successfully. This is probably not a problem to all the technically inclined, but for everybody else what are they to do. We need an easier off the shelf solution and something everyone of any age and skill level could use. I see this product going two ways!

My idea is to design a low heat battery powered crimping tool that uses heat-shrinking wrap to seal the ends of shoelaces. Sold in conjunction with a pack of cylindrical heat shrinking shoelace tips (Like what you see on common shoe laces now), all the user would have to do is cut the laces to their desired length and seal the end using this tool. No crazy tools will be needed and now you could have shoelaces at whatever length you want. No need to buy new laces. Use what you have. What I think could make this product interesting is that it could also be marketed towards kids. Imagine kids combining and juxtaposing a variety of shoelace prints and colors in interesting ways. They could make their laces customizable and have fun with their friends making their own personalized designs. This product hits multiple demographics.

The Customer

I see everyone from the young all the way to the meticulously hip wanting this product. Shoe heads, kids; anyone with an interest in fashion and their style would want this product

Key Features

  • low heat device makes crimping shoe lace ends safe for people of all ages.
  • allows for user to customize their laces in a variety of ways (length, juxtoposing colors and designs).
