An Update on Our Simple Solutions Brief

posted about 22 hours ago by baron

Four weeks ago, we announced our collaboration with Target on a new product brief, which focused on the value of cheap, everyday solutions. Over a period of seven days, members submitted nearly 1,200 ideas for simple consumer products, 15 of which will be selected by Quirky and Target to be developed and sold at retail. Which brings us to today.

Today was the day the Target team had intended to finalize their choices, but as it turns out, they ended up liking more ideas than they bargained for: 31 to be exact. As a result, we’ll have to conduct several more weeks of review before selecting the final group, and launching into the community design process. While an update is no substitute for a final decision, it’s rare that a company is hindered by an overabundance of good ideas, so we’ve got high hopes for the remainder of this project. For now, here is the full catalog of submissions on Target’s short list: while it’s not guaranteed that any one of these products will be moved into development, the final picks for the brief will come from this selection.
