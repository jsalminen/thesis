My invention is called Cat Tails Tee Shirt. It is a tee shirt that has a long catnip filled tail attached to the end of it.

Pets
Submitted by: Maxine Mensinger

The Description

It is a comfortable cotton tee shirt that has a catnip filled tail attached to the back of it. It could be customized by having a picture of the customers' cat on it as well. I got this idea when I was wearing an old tee shirt that had a hole in it. I ripped it and it formed a "tail" and when I walked it dragged behind me and my cat went nuts!

The Customer

Customers who have overweight cats who need to be exercised. Most cats can't resist string and catnip! Customers who are very busy and do not have time to play with their cats. The cat could chase the tail while the owner is walking or the cat could play with the tail while the owner is sitting.
Customers who pamper their cats and who are always looking for a new toy for them. Cats get bored with the same toys but most cats can't resist string and catnip!
