Minimalist Kitchen Disks - All-in-one kitchen tools are flat and easy to store. Great for cabin, dorm, camping or RV. RESUBMIT 350 Votes.

Kitchen
Submitted by: Debra Stone
.

The Problem

Small kitchens, dorm rooms and RV's have little storage space for all of the kitchen items needed. Items like colanders, graters, strainers, bowls, slicers, cutting boards, hot mitts and trivets take up precious cabinet, counter or other storage space. The same problem exists for cabins and camping. We need to have a way to keep these items handy, yet condense them to a manageable size for storage.

The Solution

Minimalist Kitchen Disks are a set of flat, round kitchen tools that replace multiple items in a small kitchen. All of the items are connected by a chain and can be hung up or stored together. Take off and use what you need. Pot strainer, hard cutting board, silicone pot holder/trivet, grater, collapsible bowl and colander, scraper/scooper/chopper/bottle opener, microwave bacon/pizza pan, silicone jar opener/garlic peeler and a vegetable slicer. These items are brightly colored and compact to store.

Suggestion from Kansas: Create a round one that has cooking utensils around the perimeter of the circle. Turn it one way to use it one way and turn it another direction to use it for another purpose. (Influenced update to image 2 showing scraper/scooper/chopper)

Capone suggested to add a silicone jar opener. Other optional suggestions from Capone: to add an additional tool that will act as a holder for the pieces, a pair of tongs, a handle for the multi-tool or an additional spatula/turner. The user could also possibly use it to hold up the strainer or other tools

Suggestion from Nelson Evangelista: - the chain / or the binder can be easily removed so that any of the tools can be used by itself without the other tools hanging. Ex. Microwave pans, potholder, etc. - can be shipped with a tower stack to pile it up in a neat stack on the counter when not in used

Key Features

  • Disk-shaped tools that are easy to store
  • Can be hung up or stored in a cabinet together
  • Dishwasher safe for easy cleaning.
  • Approximate size is 11" x 4"

    I want to bring up a point about storage and packing. A rectangular package is always easier to manage than a round package. In a bag, on the shelf, in your trunk or in a box: a round item will always waste volume in the corners. In addition, a bowl of the same volume is going to take about 1/3 less counter space if it's rectangular. In a small kitchen or on a folding table, that little bit makes a big difference.
    .
    I propose a kitchen tool library. Rectangular multipurpose pieces that you can mix and match to bring only what you need. Stand them or stack them. They might not all be the same thickness, but they should all be the same height and width.
