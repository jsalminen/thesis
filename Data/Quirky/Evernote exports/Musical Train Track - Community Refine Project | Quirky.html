Community Refine Project

In this project we’ll need your help choosing between three different concept directions for this product. Please vote for your favorite, and feel free to comment. 


ELECTRIC:
This concept is an electric train and track. It works by placing pins on the track that represent different music notes. The different train cars represent different instruments and make different sounds! As the train runs along the track the electric pickups underneath the train read where the pins are and play the corresponding sound!

PAPER TRACK:
This is an old school concept. The track will come equipped with a strip of paper that represents a blank sheet of music. Kids can use a hole punch to mark where on the music sheet they want the train to play. As the train runs along the track the holes trigger the mechanism and play a song!

SLIDING PINS:
Similar to the paper track, except this concept uses a system of sliding pins to mark where you want the train to play. This simple concept works by sliding the pins up and down the track (which represents a sheet of music). The train then runs along the track and the pins flick the bars on the front of the train and play a song! Kids can arrange and rearrange the pins with ease.
