The Problem

Water that is left from a shower always results in the growth of mold and bacteria. Without water, the mold and bacteria have nothing to grow on.

The Solution

By eliminating the water (FAN drying it) and using UV lights to clean and prevent mold and bacteria, showers will remain mold and germ free without labor! BLADELESS FAN rotates 180° and pushes the air against the shower walls, shower curtain, and along the tub grout to dry up remaining water after a shower. CIRCULAR UV LIGHT finishes the job by killing any remaining mold and bacteria, and prevents them from coming back. RECHARGABLE BASE UNIT has adjustable timer.
