The Description

Pivot Shower is a highly adjustable massaging showerhead. The showerhead consists of 7 high-power mini-shower nozzles that can be flexed anyway that you like. It has the potential to be a handheld shower sprayer as well as a fixed showerhead. Its unique design allows you to target one specific area or muscle group or gives you the flexibility to target multiple areas at once. Water anywhere you want it, anywhere you need it!

The graphics are not to scale! The 7 mini shower nozzles would make up the showerhead. It would be about the size of a large shower head when all the nozzles are together. The nozzles would be small enough to allow for plenty of pressure in each nozzle. Aerating high efficiency showerheads could help with low flow pressure issues. They could be moved in many shapes and configurations, to meet the needs of the individual.

The Customer

Anybody and everybody! As a recent patient recovering from neck surgery, I can relate to neck pain. My neurosurgeon explained to me that neck and shoulder pain are a widespread epidemic thanks to the digital age that we live in. People are constantly hunched over a computer (on Quirky) or looking down at other digital devices. The ability to relieve neck, shoulder, and even chest pressure can be done on a daily basis in your shower. Why not start your day or end a hectic day with a relaxing massage from your Pivot Shower!

Key Features

  • Highly adjustable
  • Relieves neck and back tension and pain

Product Comparison

  • Multiple single shower heads with flow variances, but nothing quite like this. My design gives you the flexibility to adjust 7 shower heads into virtually any shape to fit your needs.
