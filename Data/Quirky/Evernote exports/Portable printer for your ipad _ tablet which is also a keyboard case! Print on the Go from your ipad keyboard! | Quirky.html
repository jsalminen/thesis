The Problem

Having a portable printer specifically designed from ipad / tablets is surely a void in the market. How about having a sleek portable printer which can also double up as a bluetooth keyboard case? How convenient that can be !!!

The Solution

It also has a flexibility of doubling up as a keyboard for your ipad.

All these can be integrated as a case for your ipad :)
