No Brainstorm, But a Spring Cleaning Feedback Friday!

posted 23 days ago by baron

Egads, it’s almost time! Our first live Eval for the Wink: Instantly Connected product line is coming up tomorrow, and we couldn’t be more excited to get things rolling. The festivities will begin at 7pm EDT, and since the whole staff will be bustling in preparation, this means that our Wednesday brainstorm will unfortunately be cancelled this week.

However, all that brainpower certainly won’t go to waste, because we’re ending the week with a special episode of Feedback Friday (3pm EDT), focused on the wide world of cleaning products. With the Quirky cleaning line officially launching this week, the subject is surely timely, but this will hardly be your grandma’s take on cleaning. Instead, this broadcast will be all about technology: specifically, how electronics and emerging tech can be used to change the way we clean, and find untapped potential in an otherwise crowded marketplace. The panel will feature electrical engineer Nathan Meryash, along with Quirky Retail’s own Nikki Zdanow, who played a major role in the formation of Quirky’s current cleaning line. Needless to say, if you’re in the market for some expert advice, look no further than Friday.

So, how can you get involved in this special symposium? It really couldn’t be easier. Just head to the Quirky Live page at 3pm ET this Friday, and be ready with your questions! If you can’t make the broadcast, feel free to post your questions in the comments section of this post. Also, if you’d an idea of what you can expect, check out the recording of our Electronics-focused Feedback Friday below.
