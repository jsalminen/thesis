Hang Out to Dry

Hand drying certain clothes will always be a necessity, but that doesn't mean it has to be a hassle too. Grid offers a unique solution for drying garments in your own shower, providing a portable, large-scale drying rack that won't drip on your hardwood floors. Its simple format makes setup a breeze: three velcro straps secure one end of the rack to your shower rod, while suction cups secure the other end to the adjacent wall, keeping your wet clothes suspended as they drip harmlessly into the shower.
