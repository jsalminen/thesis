Behind the Scenes: Ventu Drop Testing

posted 4 months ago by baron

As the Quirky Design Staff prepares the updated Ventu for manufacturing, one of the toughest (and coolest) points to address is material composition. The main body of the bowl will be made of melamine resin, a popular plastic for kitchenware, but fine tuning the density to balance weight and durability can be a tricky task.

To see whether the latest formula made the cut, design engineer Laura Sink conducted a number of drop tests on off-tooling samples from our manufacturer. The results may surprise you…
