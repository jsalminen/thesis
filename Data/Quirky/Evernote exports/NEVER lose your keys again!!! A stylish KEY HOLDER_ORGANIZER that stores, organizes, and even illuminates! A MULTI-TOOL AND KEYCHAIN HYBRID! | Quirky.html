NEVER lose your keys again!!! A stylish KEY HOLDER/ORGANIZER that stores, organizes, and even illuminates! A MULTI-TOOL AND KEYCHAIN HYBRID!

Organization
Submitted by: germaine washington
.

The Problem

Most people carry between 4 to 8 keys with them at a time. Keys for every situation including car, house, and work. I carry at least 6 keys with me at all times. Hauling around so many keys at a time can become problematic and sometimes irritating. It seems as if I’m always fumbling with my key chain trying to find the right key for that particular situation. Key chains have always been adequate for key storage but not practical for actually use.

The Solution

One day I was in the garage messing around and using my multi-tool and I thought the multi-tool setup would be perfect for storing and organizing keys! Think about it, every key could be stored inside one device and then accessed when needed by just pulling the key out, using it and then returning it back to its original position. So I played around with the idea and created a prototype and it worked perfectly. In addition, I also liked the idea of integrating a LED light into the device. Any of us who have attempted to use a key late at night with no visibility understand the importance of this feature. This product would also include a snap for the back so the end user has the option to snap it on a belt, inside pocket, or purse strap…etc..

Key Features

  • Compact and portable
  • organizer with onboard illumination
  • Can be stored anywhere
  • organization and usability with a stylish design
  • will not require any key cutting, altering, or sending your keys out for the service
  • key will simply slide into the product and be ready for use
