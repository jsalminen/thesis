*A Multi-Functional "3 in 1" Rolling Pin for Your Garden! (Garden Roller)

Garden
Submitted by: Peter A. Wachtel
.

The Problem

When Gardening/ Planting - the 3 Main Needs are: Making Rows for your Seeds, a Rake and a Shovel. But they are cumbersome to carry around for small tasks.

The Solution

There is a Strong Correlation between those who Bake & Garden...

*The Garden Roller: Multi-Functional "3 in 1" Rolling Pin for Your Garden!

*Features:
- Rolling Seed Treads (slide to adjust the size of your seed rows) Dirt Roller Creates Rows in Dirt to Plant Seeds!
- Roller Knobs Aerate & Breakup the Dirt!
- Hand Shovel & Hand Rake. Twist & Lock Rake & Shovel in when Rolling Dirt & Store Everything Together!
- Could be made of Plastic & Silicone or Metal & Silicone 9" long.
* Could work like Twisted Sifter: http://www.quirky.com/products/316-Twisted-Sifter-flour-dusting-rolling-pin
