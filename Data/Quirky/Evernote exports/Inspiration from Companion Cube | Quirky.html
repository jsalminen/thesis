Project Brief

Community Design: Community Design Project

In this project, we want you to submit sketches, images, videos, and other visuals that illustrate industrial design directions for Denny2020’s Multipurpose Sensors.

Let’s think about different form factors. At the minimum, the unit will house power supply, a small circuit board, and the Electric Imp chip. You’ll want to think about how indicator lights, on/off switches, and mounting mechanisms might influence look of the final product. You might take inspiration from night lights, air fresheners, or adhesive hooks to start.

You’ll have 3 submissions and 3 votes in this round. We’ll use the top concepts as a starting point for our continued ideation, sketches, sketch models, etc…

After reading about the concept the first thing that came to my mind was the Companion Cube from video game Portal (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portal_(video_game)). I think a little, friendly-looking cube would be a quirky form for this product. Each side could represent different sensor, with it's own symbol or light and switch. Extra sides could be used for Quirky logo, power supply and mounting mechanisms.
