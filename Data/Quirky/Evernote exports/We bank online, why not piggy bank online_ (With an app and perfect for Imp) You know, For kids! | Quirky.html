We bank online, why not piggy bank online? (With an app and perfect for Imp) You know, For kids!

Wink
Submitted by: Jason Hunter
.

The Problem

Online banking experience for children while learning about saving money.

The Device

A piggy bank "piggy backed" with an app that keeps track of "pennies" in and "pennies" out to give kids a sense of online banking and money responsibility. Obviously recognizes all coins and I suggest a dollar bill slot as well. This app will be able to set goals and show progress.

App Features

  • Sets goals
  • Set alerts toward goals
  • Instant up dates
  • Online bank environment
