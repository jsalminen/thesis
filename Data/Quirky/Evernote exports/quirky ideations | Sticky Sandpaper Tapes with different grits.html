Project Brief

Product Design: Community Design Phase

In this project we want you to submit sketches, images, videos, and other visuals that illustrate design directions for Felipe Gonzalez’s Sandpaper Sticks.

We will take the top ideas as inspiration towards the final design. Please take a look at the original submission (linked above) before you get to work!
