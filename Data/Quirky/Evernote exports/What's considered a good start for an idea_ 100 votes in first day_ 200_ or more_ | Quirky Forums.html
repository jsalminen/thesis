Nathaniel Padgett | 09/11/2012 | 02:20 PM

Votes help with an ideas visibility, but are not the deciding factor in whether or not we will choose to make it. After some time we recognized that ideators were recruiting votes from people who liked them, but didn't necessarily have an opinion on the product idea being proposed. So, we switched to making the decisions internally and reviewing ideas on their individual merit, rather than solely on the votes they received.
.
@Andy - you second idea hasn't been live for 2 days, so it's not eligible for UC yet. Congrats on such a high number of votes in a short period of time! For those who don't know, we impose a 48 hour restriction on staff and Pro members placing an idea under consideration, so the rest of the community can comment and vote on it.

.
