Painter buddy slides thru ladder rung and locks in place to hold your paint can and brush, putty knife, elec sander etc. Has magnetic strips

Other
Submitted by: Bassfishing
  
  

The Description

The painter buddy assists the ladder user in minimizing the constant up and down the ladder to fetch his needed supplies. It will provide two hooks on the end of a 3"x5" platform to hold the paint can and extra hooks to hang items such as paint brushes and power tools. It also has magnetic strips that will hold screwdrivers and other metal objects. Or just make it simple and hold a paint can and putty knife only.

  

The Customer

Anyone in the painting industry.

General ladder users.

Homeowners.
