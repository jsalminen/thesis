…And We’re Back, with a Momentous Town Meeting Recap

posted about 1 month ago by baron

Well makers, after a week of rest and recuperation, the Quirky team is back at QHQ, which means that the quest for accessible invention has restarted with renewed strength and spirit. The second quarter of 2013 will be one of unprecedented hustle, starting with a groundbreaking (and we really mean groundbreaking) announcement this Wednesday, April 10th, so keep your hearts and minds firmly set on this date. From that point forward, everything is going to change.

However, in the meantime, there’s no shortage of stuff to talk about, starting with last week’s momentous Town Meeting. On top of the usual staff recaps and statistics, we unveiled a massive company-wide rebranding project, which will redefine Quirky’s public image as the year progresses. We’ll be sharing more info on the rebranding in the coming weeks, but if you didn’t tune in for last Friday’s broadcast, we highly recommend that you check out the recording below. That is, unless you LIKE being out of the loop…

So, to recap:

Looking Back: An historic Town Meeting, and a new face for Quirky

Looking Forward: A game-changing announcement this Wednesday, April 10th (stay tuned for more info)
