Now you can clean (scrape} grille rods on both the top and bottom of your barbecue grille without having to take the grille out. #spotsv

Accessories
Submitted by: paul ortega
.

The Problem

Most grille scrapers just clean the top of the grille. If you want to clean the bottom, one has to take out the grille and flip it over to clean the bottom side.

The Solution

This grille scraper is inserted between the grill rods then turned 1/4 turn to fit between the grille rods. A back and forth motion with a slight angle will clean both the bottom and top. The V cut on the scraper cleans the top only of a v shape grille rod. This idea can be wider with the notches cut deeper to clean between narrow grille rods.

Key Features

  • cleans both sides of grille without having to take grille out to clean the other side.
