RESUB : Lens cap holder with clip on system instead of strap-on holder.

Accessories
Submitted by: DianaW

The Problem

The lens cap holders with exist at the moment are all strap-on holders. I usually don't use the strap because it gets in my way.

The Solution

This is a holder with a clip-on system. You can clip it on anything your like (in your bag, on your bag, on your jacket). It will get even more perfect when the diameter is adjustable as well, so it will be suitable for any make an model and you don't have to a new holder when you have a new make lens or new camera. Don't have the right picture yet (1st picture is of strap-on holder).

It's a bit like a ski pass holder (2nd picture). Can even be with a pull out cord as well, pull the cord out, click the lens cap holder on it and let it back in.

Key Features

  • Clip on holder instead of strap-on holder.
  • Suitable for any make and model.
  • Never have a lens cap lost again !
