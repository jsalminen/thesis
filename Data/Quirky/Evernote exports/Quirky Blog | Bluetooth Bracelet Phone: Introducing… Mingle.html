Bluetooth Bracelet Phone: Introducing… Mingle

posted 10 days ago by baron

The bluetooth headset is a handy tool, but with its current status as a pop culture punching bag, its benefits barely outweigh the cost. Mingle is an accessory with a different approach, packing a full bluetooth handset into a slim and stylish bracelet. When dormant, its simple form rests discreetly on your wrist, with a spring-loaded hinge to keep it firmly in place. However, when you receive a call, subtle lights and vibrations notify you instantly, and a quick flip transforms Mingle into a fully functional handset, letting you take the call with no rummaging required. You’ll also look great doing it, but hey, you didn’t need our help with that anyway.

After watching countless women tear through their bags in search of a ringing phone, inventor Steve Caplin decided that a simpler solution was in order, and set out to design a bluetooth handset that was as fun to wear as it was to use. Two months and 250 community influencers later, that idea transformed into Mingle. Head to Quirky’s Upcoming section to learn more about our latest launch, and to help us determine its price!

Features:
– Discreet phone handset doubles as a bracelet
– Bluetooth connectivity links with any phone
– Vibration and subtle light notify you of incoming calls
– Spring-loaded hinge wraps firmly around your wrist
– Locking mechanism stays open during calls
– Dock for charging and storage included
– Low battery warning light

Materials:
– Plastic
– Metal

Dimensions:
– 80mm W x 80mm H x 35mm D

Price, qty and features subject to change during production process.
