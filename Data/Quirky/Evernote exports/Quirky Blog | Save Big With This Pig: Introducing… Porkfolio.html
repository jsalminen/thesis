Save Big With This Pig: Introducing… Porkfolio

posted 2 days ago by Nathaniel

Few products are more iconic than the piggy bank, but nowadays, secure storage alone just doesn’t cut it. Introducing Porkfolio, an app-enabled piggy bank that loads a full financial institution into one lovable package. When a coin drops in, Porkfolio’s nose will light up, and the increase will be pushed to the partner app, letting you track your balance and set savings goals on the fly. A built-in accelerometer lets you know if someone tampers with your stash, which can only be unlocked with the app or a special input code. Isn’t it time you saved smarter?

Inventor Jason Hunter‘s idea was so loved by the Quirky staff that we Evaled it twice before making him an inventor. Now after 330 influencers helped the Quirky Design Staff perfect the concept, Porkfolio is finally ready for the big time. Head over to the Upcoming page today to check out it out for yourself and help us choose the perfect price in the process.

Features:
– Stores and identifies any U.S. coin
– Account balance on app is instantly updated when money is saved
– Snout lights up in different colors when various coins are inserted
– Moving limbs and head make Porkfolio fully posable
– Accelerometer lets you know if Porkfolio is being tampered with
– Integrated lock provides security, and can only be unlocked through the app, or with five physical shakes
– Head detaches to empty stash

Materials:
– Soft-to-touch plastic
– Final finishes may vary

Dimensions:
210mm L x 210mm W x 136mm D

Price, qty and features subject to change during production process.
