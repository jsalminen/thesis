So Fresh and So Clean: Meet the Quirky Cleaning Line

posted 30 days ago by baron

(left to right):Scrubbie, Nest, Squeak, Flipside, Broom Groomer v2, Groove, Broom Groomer Pro

Over the past week, you’ve probably noticed that our product launches have shared a certain sanitary flair. This is no coincidence, but the build-up to a rather exciting announcement: we’re launching our first complete line of cleaning products, and boy does it look great.

The Quirky Cleaning Line was designed as an entirely new take on cleaning, and incorporates a wide array of different products, both old and new. Some of them are fresh from the workshop, like the three-sided mop Scrubbie, while others, like the dishwashing multi-tool Groove, have been a longer time coming. The set even includes a fresh take on a classic: the redesigned Broom Groomer v2, now with a broom to keep it company. The result is a comprehensive suite of products designed to work in tandem, improving your life through a redefined vision of what it means to clean.

This idea of reinvention permeates every aspect of the line, from form and function to even color. When the Quirky Design Staff started work on this project, they wanted to challenge existing attitudes towards cleaning, harnessing versatility and ease-of-use to make the task enjoyable rather than tedious. All seven products are designed to be light and neat, using simple forms and aluminum poles to shed the weightiness associated with conventional cleaning products, while maintaining a high standard of quality. The color scheme was also intentionally bright, forgoing the stark, austere greys and pure whites that are all too common in the cleaning aisle. Combined with a unique design ethos, versatile yet simple, these elements craft a complete user experience that anyone can appreciate, ensuring that the user always has the right tool on hand, and dare we say it, has fun as well.

While tooling and manufacturing is still underway, we can’t wait to get this stuff on store shelves, and the entire cleaning line should be out on the market before Summer’s end. We’ll keep you up-to-date on what comes next, but in the meantime, we hope you’re as psyched about this exciting new line as we are! Which one is your favorite?
