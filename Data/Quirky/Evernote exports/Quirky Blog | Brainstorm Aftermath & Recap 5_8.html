Brainstorm Aftermath & Recap 5/8

posted 10 days ago by anna

We had an all-Wink Brainstorm this week, featuring the first three GE Eval selections and a brand new cast of Quirky characters (meet them here). All three ideas discussed incorporate an Imp chip, which (in laymen’s terms) allows any object to connect to Wifi. Thanks to the Imp, the capabilities for these products are endless. We had to keep forcing ourselves to imagine how homes of the future might be with these babies. Read on and get a glimpse into a Jetson-like future.

Product 608: Refrigerator Camera
When Inventor David Lent submitted his idea for the refrigerator camera in 2011, he envisioned a way to time a snapshot with the opening of the fridge door to capture visual inventory of your groceries. QDS and the Wink team explored challenges around power, connectivity, and camera angles in this week’s brainstorm.

Product 596: Smart Alarm Clock
Ryan Pendleton‘s minimalist alarm clock idea allows synced calendars and online information to adjust your daily wake up time. It’s an elegant way to utilize your smartphone alerts without disrupting your sleep sanctuary. We had lots of fun thinking about customizable ringtones and passive interaction with personal technology. Watch the full discussion here.

Product 609: Door Camera
Inventor Denny Fong‘s Multipurpose Sensors were once again in the spotlight as we imagined ways to use the doorbell as a trigger for various security and convenience features in the entryway. There’s huge potential in this space, so let’s see how our new Wink engineers work their way in, out, and about the doorway to the connected home. Watch the full discussion here.
