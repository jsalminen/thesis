Tool Cube - 100% Useful - Inside and Out.

Organization
Submitted by: Constantine
.

The Problem

Finding space to store, organize and locate your tools and equipment.

The Solution

Tool cube is a storage cabinet on the inside, and four-sided combination magnetic and nano-suction tool board on the outside. The top is a cabinet door. Organizes all your tools in one place. The Cube secures anything you place on it or in it (ferrous or nonferrous).

For all your heavier tools and equipment, the Cube top opens to reveal a storage cabinet. In short, The Tool Cube is your central tooling hub. Every square inch of this cube is useful - inside and out. The top may be used as a work bench too.

- Suggestions Welcome -

Please write your suggestions in the space provided for 'Explanation Of Your Changes'. Do not write in my original sections - thanks.

xrisxross suggests: Make it a rotating box on a cylindrical stand this way if you want to use a tool you just spin it to get to the side of the box that the tool you want is located. Also it should have a transparent cover on a hinge and a lock system for protection for children and safe keeping of your tools. Tools are expensive these days. I would also like to see a light source on the all sides and inside the cabinet to be able to see the tools you want on those days when am working late in the garage.

Evonne Wilks suggests some drawers for small things.

Key Features

  • ~30-inches on each side.
  • Storage cabinet (not shown)
  • 4-sided magnetic and nano-suction tool board.
  • Top work bench and cabinet opening.
  • Locking caster wheels (not shown).
  • Handle to push around on wheels (not shown)
