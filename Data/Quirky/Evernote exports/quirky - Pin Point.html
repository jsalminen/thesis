Pin Point

Click and Stick

Imagine how much time and finger pricking you could save yourself if you didn’t have to reach into a box of push pins when you need to pin something up. Pin Point helps you avoid all of that. It’s a push pin pen that allows you to safely store, push, and reload pins like a ninja, without jeopardizing your fingertips. Slide the button forward to push individual pins into a surface and slide it back to prime another. The back end of Pin Point opens to allow easy reloading, and each pin features a male and female side, so they can easily stack on top of one another.

Imagine how much time and finger pricking you could save yourself if you didn’t have to reach into a box of push pins when you need to pin something up. Pin Point helps you avoid all of that. It’s a push pin pen that allows you to safely store, push, and reload pins like a ninja, without jeopardizing your fingertips. Slide the button forward to push individual pins into a surface and slide it back to prime another. The back end of Pin Point opens to allow easy reloading, and each pin features a male and female side, so they can easily stack on top of one another.

Features:
 - Slide button for pushing in and reloading pins
 - Pen clip, so it’s right where you need it
 - 12 stack-able pushpins

Materials:
 - Plastic
– Metal

Dimensions:
 - 146mm H x 20mm W x 26mm D
