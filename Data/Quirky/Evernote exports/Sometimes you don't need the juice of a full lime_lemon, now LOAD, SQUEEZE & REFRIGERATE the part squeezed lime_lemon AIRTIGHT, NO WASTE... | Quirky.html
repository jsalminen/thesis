Sometimes you don't need the juice of a full lime/lemon, now LOAD, SQUEEZE & REFRIGERATE the part squeezed lime/lemon AIRTIGHT, NO WASTE...

Kitchen
Submitted by: Lonr

The Problem

Sometimes you just don't need the juice of a full lime or lemon, and the part squeezed lime or lemon, unless refrigerated airtight, gets dehydrated and goes to waste.

The Solution

A lime/lemon squeezer that allows for part squeezed lime/lemon to be refrigerated airtight. It consists of a compartment into which the lime/lemon is slit and loaded. The top portion that has a fold up winding handle with a plunger mechanism and an airtight seal is screwed on. Turing the handle pushes the plunger downward and squeezes the lime/lemon and the juice passes through the holes in the bottom of the compartment. When done, the lid with an airtight seal is screwed on to the bottom to make it airtight, the handle is folded up and the part squeezed lime/lemon is refrigerated airtight. No dehydration, no wastage.

Can be completely dismantled for dishwasher.
