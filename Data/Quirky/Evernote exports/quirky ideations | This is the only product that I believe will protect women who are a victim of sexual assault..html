The Description

In the United States alone there are 207,754 victims (age 12 or older) of rape and sexual assault each year. That means every 2 minutes, someone in the U.S. is sexually assaulted. To me, this is a scary number which I would like to see drop. I believe the product that I am about to present should help reduce the amount of victims being sexually assaulted and will ensure that they leave the scene harmless.

 

The Customer

The vision that I have for this product is a bracelet that is disguised as a taser. The reason why I think that it should be a bracelet in disguise is because you don't have to worry about pulling it out of your purse if an attacker was to approach you. You also ensure that the attacker is not aware that you are caring a taser. If an attacker approach's you, all you have to do is press a button discreetly on your bracelet and this will activate the taser. Once the attacker grabs you, simply touch him with your wrist which you have the bracelet on and the attacker will be mobilized giving you the opportunity to get away and call for help.

 

Key Features

  • You have it on you at all times. No need to look for it in your purse when you are being attacked by the attacker.
