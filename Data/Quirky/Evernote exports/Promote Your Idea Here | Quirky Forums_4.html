Nathaniel Padgett | 09/08/2012 | 03:50 PM

So here's my though on pimping threads:
.
First, the term pimping just sounds terrible and doesn't exactly encourage association. Why would anyone want to join a discussion about pimping? I can think of some reasons, none of which relate to inventing. Promotion Zone, "Hype Your Idea", Idea Zone, Invention Zone, "TAKE A LOOK AT MY IDEA!", would all be more meaningful to people.
.
Second, a thread in which everyone promotes their ideas won't be effective at helping any one get noticed, since most go unanswered and are quickly pushed below new ones. This eventually leads to general promotion throughout the forum and the creation of more threads dedicated to it. The Participate page and your personal social networks are the best ways to promote your idea. The former especially will be more useful once we introduce a new idea scroll.
