Break the limits in customization and make all your electronic green!

With the solar panel kit, you can
* Choose the amount of energy your want (Each solar cell tells you the avg amount of energy it can generate). Pick no of cells.
* Choose the size and shape of your cell. (Kit has different shaped cells - triangular, square, rectangular etc, of different areas, with energy engraved in them)
* Refer manual to create some cool shapes, or if you are creative make your own shapes.
* Choose you shapes such that they fit any electronic equipment and stick it to any portion on the equipment, or on your window/ car, outdoor etc.
You could even make 3D shapes with it.
Note: Sticking material is provided
* Based on what which device you want to make solar, choose the kind of connector (eg: USB)
