Power in Any Position: Introducing… Prop Power Rugged

posted 9 days ago by baron

When you’re installing wall fixtures at a busy worksite, the last thing you want to worry about is dragging a power cord. Introducing Prop Power Rugged, a heavy-duty version of the upcoming Prop Power, with a flexible neck that can wrap around ladders, scaffolds, or most anything else, ensuring that outlets are never far away. Its twelve-foot cable gives it room to roam, and three grounded outlets provide enough power for any project. All this goodness is encased in a sturdy shell, with a rugged construction that’s design to be as resilient as you are. If only “thoughtful” and “tough” came together more often.

In 2013, inventor Jacob Riesgaard came to Quirky with an idea called “Power Snake”, which allowed busy workers to attach their outlets where they needed them. One year and thousands of community influencers later, Prop Power is on its way to retail shelves, and with the introduction of Prop Power Rugged, we’re proud to announce that it won’t be going alone. Head to Quirky’s Upcoming section to learn more about our latest launch, and to help us determine its price.

Features:
– Flexible neck wraps around ladders, scaffolds, etc.
– 12 foot extension cord for maximum mobility
– Rugged construction ideal for worksites and outdoor projects
– 3 grounded outlets provide plentiful power
– 120 V

Materials:
– Rigid plastic body
– Flexible thermoplastic elastomer sheath

Dimensions:
– Cord: 365cm L (12 ft.)
– Neck: 440mm L (18 in.)
– Outlet: 114mm L x 51mm W x 32mm D

Price, qty and features subject to change during production process.
