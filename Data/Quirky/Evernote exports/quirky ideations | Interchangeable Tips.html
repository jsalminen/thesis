Project Brief

Product Design: Refine Phase

  

It’s time to refine John Rakowski’s  Multi Purpose Grapnel  submission. We’ve reviewed your comments and submissions from the  Community Design Phase  and taken them into consideration for the following refined concepts. (Don’t worry, we will award influence for any ideas that helped inform our refinement phase and the final product.)

Please vote for the concept idea you like, If you have something to say, or have new ideas that could improve this product idea, please don’t hesitate to comment! Or take it a step further and submit a new concept, building off of the directions we are exploring.
