One of the most important things the new naming system controls is the effect of self interest on the voting.

When we know that we will be rewarded for guessing what we think the staff likes instead of expressing what we like, the results will be guesses about what the staff likes.

Rather than go back to showing us many of the names, then rewarding us if we guess well, Quirky needs to expand this type of system to all of the rounds.

An improvement the staff can make to the new naming system is to make sure the awarding of influence % is disconnected from the results.
Since Q now controls which names we vote for, Q can no longer reward us based on which names we vote for.
