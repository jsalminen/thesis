RiceForOne is a scale to help you, the bachelor/ette, prepare single portion of rice with the right consistency.

Kitchen
Submitted by: Dogan Sekercioglu
.

The Problem

Preparing rice in small portions and in right consistency is always a problem for the non-cooking type. Don't you feel guilty every time you throw a big chunk of rice into the trash?

The Solution

What if you have a scale that helps you to get a single (up to four) portion of rice and the right amount for water required to cook it? RiceForOne helps the user to wash and measure 1 unit of rice to 1.5 units of water, which is the most common proportion to prepare medium cooked rice, in one product.

Key Features

  • Simple, cheap and convenient.

Product Comparison

  • Pasta portion guides, measuring cups, strainers
