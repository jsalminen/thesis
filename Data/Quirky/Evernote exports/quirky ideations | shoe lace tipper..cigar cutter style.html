    Take the cigar cutter.... it may seem is useless... why not just bite the end off? ... it's about style... this shoelace device must have style it will sell in upmarket shoe shops... to people who have $5,000 worth of shoes.. waving a hair dryer around and using a box cutter may be effective... but it lacks style.. this product won't be cheap it's a luxury item.. This design is a two stage squeeze first heat then squeeze again to final cut the end of the lace.

    The scything action of the blade means a clean cut the front can be unscrewed to replace the blade..power two AA batteries in handle,,, heat coil a high electrically resistant wire in heat proof plastic.will get hotin 15 seconds..
