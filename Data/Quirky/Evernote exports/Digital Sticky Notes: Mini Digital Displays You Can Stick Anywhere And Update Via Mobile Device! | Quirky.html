Digital Sticky Notes: Mini Digital Displays You Can Stick Anywhere And Update Via Mobile Device!

Wink
Submitted by: Kansas

The Problem

An easier way to leave reminders for people or yourself. Replaces the clutter and waste left by traditional Post-It Notes with something that can be updated and changed on the fly.

The Device

Digital Sticky Notes utilizes E ink paper display (or similar) and would be updated via an app from your mobile device. These small digital displays would be designed to stick on surfaces where most people would typically find them: refrigerators, front doors, bathroom mirrors, car dashboards, etc. Basically you would stick them anywhere you would normally stick an ordinary Post-It. They would be adhered to surfaces using double stick tape, Velcro, suction cups, or possibly Geckskin and could potentially be sold in multiple packs of six. ************************************************************************

Suggestion from Sebastien Lauzon: Why not also have a small play/record button so you could send voice memo or listen to voice memo in addition to the digital sticky notes. From the App, have it play an alarm or make it flash so it's easier to locate. Should be waterproof, could have a built-in GPS so you can locate them.

Suggestion from Suddenhalt: Paper dyed in Stontium Aluminate would glow in the dark so place your phones face over it then count to ten till image is absorbed by the dye, if it were tyvek it could not be ripped. You could write on it with a light from your camera too. It could be reused over 300 times. Different colors are available too.

Suggestion from Anant: Better sticking by adding glue to top and bottom both. We don't want a digital device to fall down.

Suggestion from Richa: Rub it off with your finger rubbing the screen of your cell (in display mode) and this will rub off the same in the digital note. (gestures)

App Features

  • The app would display your sticky notes and their location. Simply click on one of the notes to create or hit the 'X' to clear.
  • The app would enable you to use sketch and draw your notes or use the Qwerty keyboard.
  • The app would allow to choose the background color of the sticky note and the color of ink.
