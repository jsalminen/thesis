As of 8/6/2012: The ideator of Pivot Power (inventor Jake Zein) has been paid $126,000 (roughly 38% of the total payout, which is typical). He is by far the highest paid. The average idea has made between $6,000-$9,000 per year in stores. The lowest paid idea is Pedal Drops which has only paid out $1,600 over 2.2 years. Quirky has paid out a total (for all products) of $1,331,630. In case anyone was wondering the next highest paying idea (after Pivot Power) has earned the inventor $40,000.
