Whats the point of following, or being followed?

So I spent most of last week creating an idea a day and reacquainting myself with sketchup. I spent most of this week refining the ideas, re-rendering my shoddy graphics and tweaking my pages.

In that time I've picked up 22 'followers'. Why?

I now think 4 of my ideas look really good, but you know what? I have absolutely no idea. After a week, no one is going to look at my ideas again, not until Quirky staff put me in UC or the time runs out. I may as well forget trying to 'pimp' my new renders here.

Why aren't my followers shown my new renders? I guess there might be a miracle and someone pops over to give me their opinion but that's unlikely.

I also deleted most of my earlier pictures since I want anyone checking out a design to get the best impression, so no one has any idea of how much work I did, or what my process was. Not even someone who has already voted.

Quirky should work more like Facebook surely? I make contacts, vote on their ideas, make suggestions, get informed when they update their design, give feedback, tell my other friends about their better idea, get them to vote, see what they're voting on etc, etc. I have no idea what the people I'm 'following' are up to, and I'm not going to go and check every page.

As it is now, you add an idea, get some votes for a few days, maybe a suggestion.... and that's it... until you go into UC or 30 days has passed.

The only community is in the forum it seems, where is the Quirky Wall?
