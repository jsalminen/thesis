The Problem

Usually, with your classic teapot, when you want to stop the tea infusion, you have to remove the tea leaves container from the teapot.

The Solution

Welcome to the future...
With this new transparent teapot project, you just have to change its position, in order to stop tea infusion, in a move.
The tea leaves container keep its position inside.
You can serve your tea in an easy way.
You prefer strong tea, no problem, just go back in first position, for a new infusion.

It could be made of glass.
