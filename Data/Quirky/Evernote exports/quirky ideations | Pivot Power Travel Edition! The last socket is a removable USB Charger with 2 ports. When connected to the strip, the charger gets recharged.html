Pivot Power Travel Edition! The last socket is a removable USB Charger with 2 ports. When connected to the strip, the charger gets recharged

Electronics
Submitted by: Brandon Murray

The Problem

When people are on the go, it's harder to get the power needed for electronics. If you're staying in a hotel, you are limited to the room's outlets and using your laptop. I also had another similar idea lately I call "Travel Power" here: http://www.quirky.com/ideations/281478

The Solution

I propose making a Pivot Power with a removable/rechargeable USB Charger. This would mean people would have the power they need no matter where they are, or what they're doing!

Another option is to only have 3 power sockets, and the USB charger, making it a 4 socket strip. So it would be smaller for traveling. But a 6 socket strip is fine too. The USB Charger would be very handy either way. Also I would consider maybe making the USB socket with a different color faceplate, to further distinguish it from the power sockets.

Key Features

  • Has a removable and rechargeable USB Charger
  • Has the Pivot Power design we all know and love
