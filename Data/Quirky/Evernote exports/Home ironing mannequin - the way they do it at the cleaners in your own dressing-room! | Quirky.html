The Problem

Are you sick of ironing all the time? Is it a long process which requires you to be there all the time and to be extra careful not to burn your favorite blouse? Do you pay your maid to iron or do you iron your clothes at the cleaning shop? That sound like a big investment in your clothing!

The Solution

A mannequin which has small pores on the side and which is filled with water. When turned on it releases water vapors which automatically iron the clothing on it - just like they do it at the professional shop, now in your own home! It lowers your cost and/or your work for the same results you would get at the cleaners.
