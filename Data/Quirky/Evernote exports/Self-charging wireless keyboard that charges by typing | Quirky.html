Self-charging wireless keyboard that charges by typing

Electronics
Submitted by: Geoffery
.

The Problem

Wireless keyboards can run out of battery power at the worst possible time. However a self-charging keyboard will always be at full power.

The Solution

Every keystroke generates just enough to energy to keep your wireless keyboard charged and ready for continuous, uninterrupted typing.

Additionally, a solar capable keyboard could be used to absorb ambient office or room lighting to generate even more power.

- LuckyAnant suggests:
"Instead of having this setup in every key (which maybe expensive), have it in the most used keys (eg: Spacebar, delete, maybe enter)."

Key Features

  • Eliminates the need for replacing batteries
