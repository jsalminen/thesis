Spin to Plug In: Introducing… OneEighty

posted 5 days ago by baron

From media center to server to a teacher’s best friend, Apple’s Mac Mini has set a new standard for versatility. That is, until you try to reach around it for a cable. Get the most out of your Mini with OneEighty, a swiveling stand that lets you access all your vital cables with a simple twist of the wrist. Just pop OneEighty into your Mac Mini’s base, weave your cables through the built-in cord management, and your Mini will be free to roam, locking at both 90 and 180 degrees for effortless access. Whether you’re mounting on a desk, a wall, or anywhere else, OneEighty might turn the Mac Mini into your favorite Apple product.

Inventor Paul Patrocky loves his Mac Mini, but can’t stand its rear port placement, so in typical Quirky fashion, he set out to invent a solution of his own. His simple accessory concept was a hit at live Eval, and with the help of 1301 community influencers, Paul’s days of Mac Mini frustration are numbered. Head to Quirky’s Upcoming section to learn more about our latest launch, and to help us determine its price!

Features:
– Easy access to Mac Mini ports via swiveling base
– Locking mechanism locks rotation at 90 and 180 degrees
– Built-in cord management for all your Mac Mini’s cables
– Replaces existing Mac Mini base
– Desk and wall mounting options

Materials:
– Polypropylene

Dimensions:
– 197mm W x 197mm H x 17.5mm D

Price, qty and features subject to change during production process.
