A NEEDED DEVICE!! that attaches to your bed rail that will let up and down when needed to help a person get out of bed independantly.

Other
Submitted by: Rhonda Killian

The Description

I am 51 years old and after having hip surgery I needed help getting out of bed. I was using a chair beside my bed and would grab the post of the chair to help pull myself out of the bed. I got tired of the chair being in the way. I wanted to get out of bed independently, so we invented this device that we call the helping hand bed post which attaches to the bed rail.

The Customer

I know for myself, people who have had hip and back surgery need this device. Also, the ederly, handicap, injured and perhaps obessed people. I have used this for 4 months and have proven it to be successful.

Key Features

  • This light weight, portable device is easily assembled in less than 5 minutes. It will let down and raise up when needed so you can make your bed without it being visible in your bedroom.

Product Comparison

  • The only thing is the full size hospital bed rail in which keeps a person from falling out of the bed. Nothing to our knowledge is out there to help a person get out of bed independantly.
