'Psshh' was the sound made when Wharton slapped me with his dainty glove.


It was then, that the gods forged a crown of Molybdenum, The sky divided with a noise unspeakable, a colour indescribable and the crown of rare metal suspended above our heads.


A silent voice whispered directly to our inner selves. ..it said "The Duelists Crown."


A list of rules followed.



  • A ThreadDuel has two or more participants.

  • They agree on a theme.

  • They set a deadline.

  • After scoring, the highest average score wins, ..everyone else loses.

  • Duel entries cannot be submitted to a contest

  • The winner displays the Duelist Crown in their profile, the previous winner removes it.

  • The loser(s) must use an avatar made by the winner for a specified period of time.

  • Other stakes can be made and agreed on by all duelists.

  • The winner gets first choice at next challenger.

  • These duels should be well paced, to avoid long waits between duels.

  • Use this tag in your submission

  • Other duels may take place concurrent to the main duel, ..however, only the main duel passes the Duelists Crown.

  • You may not duel while you have a losers avatar

  • Current crown holder must accept duel within a week of receiving it

  • Failure to meet deadline is ALWAYS a loss

  • If all duelists in a crown duel fail to meet deadline, the crown goes to the winner of the next finishing duel.

  • Promotion is very limited, ..you may post your design here when scoring, and in the official Threadless promo blog only.

  • In the event of a tied score, the submission with the most 5's wins.
