melmike
on May 27, 2013 at 06:42:25
2 designs submitted - Score now! Quote

Having never used it before, I wasn't very smart. I just recorded as I drew, stupidly thinking that I could set the playback rate to a higher speed later on. Then I found out that my version of Camtasia doesn't do that. I just downloaded the newer version though, which looks like it might.

So I had all these hours of raw recordings, and then had to re-record it bit by bit as I manually dragged the playback slider across so it looked like it was playing fast. The end result was pretty close to others I see online.

For my next one I'm going to do some tests beforehand. I'll try recording at a low frame rate, and also check out the clip-speed functions in the new Camtasia 7. I suppose what will look best will depend on how many hours you record.

But really, once you've made your recordings, editing and exporting is super easy.

One tip I would say is to keep your window the same size as you work. That'll save so much time when you're putting it all together.
