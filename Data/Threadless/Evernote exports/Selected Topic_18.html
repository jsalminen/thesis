Why did my design finish scoring early?

Your submission can be dropped from the running before the end of the 7 day voting period if it receives a low score within the first 24 hours of voting. This helps keep the number of submissions manageable for voters and lets artists whose designs are scoring poorly know early on so they can take their work in a new direction.
