So, I've been thinking for a while about a Swanson portrait made from actual eggs and bacon - I decided to give it a shot a couple of days ago- with the ultimate plan of giving to my husband for breakfast- by the time all was said and done, it was not that appetizing anymore. As one might imagine, eggs and bacon are not the easiest media- I changed a coupla things in phtoshop and am now at this point.


Obviously there is a lot of work left (shadow fixing,etc.), but I'm trying to figure out if it is worth it. I was super psyched until I showed my hubs this photo and he is not a fan.


Should I continue treating it as a photo, make it more digital, or just scrap the idea and be happy the dogs got the meal of the century?


I have lots of photos, including some with a fork, so there is the potential for a bunch of changes.
