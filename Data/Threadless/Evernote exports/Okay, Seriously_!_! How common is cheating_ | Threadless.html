Okay, Seriously?!?! How common is cheating?

  • by KKimball318
  • posted Jun 29, 2013 in general
  • 14 Comments

I am not usually one to nitpick or care what other people are doing, but I seriously wondered how this awful design became one of the highest scoring: http://www.threadless.com/baseballtees/since-1839/
So I looked at the comments. Two of them have profile pictures of actress Ava Sambora (http://www.threadless.com/profile/2560509/siskanur, http://www.threadless.com/profile/2560495/angelana) both of whom have scored one design with an average score of 5.00. One of them is comedian Ava Vidal (http://www.threadless.com/profile/2560536/selesei) who has scored 17 submissions giving an average score of 2.00. Now, ignoring the fact that the majority of these fake profiles have ridiculously foreign names and the inability to speak english despite the fact that they look very European/North American, the coupe de grace is probably this: http://www.threadless.com/profile/2560562/bellaaamanda. The profile who's picture is a little girl who can apparently write and purchase things online. Her name is Ava and she is found only one other place on the web, here: http://www.averagebetty.com/blog/twitter-grazing/


Now I realize that the Threadstaff would never ever pick that design. But what bothers me is the fact that several of these fake accounts were used to downvote 32 other designs and give them ones. Is there a way for the Threadstaff to remove both these accounts and their scores? How common is cheating? I'm new to Threadless but it seems like it happens a lot.
