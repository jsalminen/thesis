i think it's probably not an ideal situation when both parties are so similar that they are actually competing against each other rather than learning from the other, or complementing the other.

if you're both artists, it can be a learning and encouraging experience. but that all depends on your personalities. if you're both highly competitive, it could be motivating at first, but tiring and defeating in the long run.

i also believe that, although it is important to have similar interests, it's also important to like different things. otherwise things might get a little boring if you can't be learning new things from eachother. on the other hand, if you like all the same things, you can take new adventures together.
