ThreadDuels is, as JeffreyG put it, "the Dirty hoodrat version of Threadwars."


Well here is the Dirty hoodrat version of Remake!!


Swapsies?
In this challenge, you find a partner, and remake something from their unprinted subs. ..they remake one of yours ...That's it!


Maybe your idea reinterpreted by someone else, or their idea in your style is exactly what those subs needed.


What are the rules? I'm all about rules.
There's no over-arching deadline, just find a partner, swap subs, set your own personal timeframe between you and have fun! ..make mention of your partner and subs chosen and I'll include it here.


Also, mention the challenge in your sub description, add partner as a collaboratorwhen you submit. The design and include the Remake swapsies logo, to evade those comments along the lines of "Someone did this already you dumb dummy!, stop being dumb, you dumb guy."


Logo to include in your subs


Splitting Winnings?
Splitting is entirely up to you, a basic agreement would be 50/50 if either is printed.
You could organise something else, but is that teaming up? ...is it?
