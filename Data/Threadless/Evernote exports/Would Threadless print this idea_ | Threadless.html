So I had this idea of Moses coming down with two iPad tablets with the ten commandments on them. He'd be holding the tablets out triumphantly, with some ethereal light coming from behind him. Moses would also be wearing a black turtle neck over his raggy clothing.


But when I googled to see if this concept had been done, I found a few lamely photoshopped versions of it, and a few newspaper comics with depictions of it. (none with a sweater)


So my question is would threadless really consider it if the idea wasn't original in the sense that other people have done it, though I came up with the joke wholly on my own.
