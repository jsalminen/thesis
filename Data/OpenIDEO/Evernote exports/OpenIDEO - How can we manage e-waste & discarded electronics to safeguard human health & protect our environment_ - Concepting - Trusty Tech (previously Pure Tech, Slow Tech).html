Trusty Tech (previously Pure Tech, Slow Tech)

Creating a label for long lasting technology.
 
Pure tech, label
 

This simple concept is inspired by the slow food movement and organic farmers I  visited during my trip to South Tirol.

The idea is to promote companies which develop long lasting products. The "pure tech" ("slow tech") label will support this movement. Quality criteria will be set up which measure the durability of the electronic devices.

Pilot Companies will show the profitability of long lasting and sustainable technology.

I´m happy to discuss the first draft of this concept with you.

 
 

How does your concept safeguard human health and protect our environment?

The promotion of long lasting products will decrease the amount of e-waste.
 

Where does your concept fit into the lifecycle of electronic devices?

This concept inspires manufacturers to design products that last longer.
 

What steps could be taken today to start implementing your concept?

The branding of the label can start today, then criteria to get the label and first pilot companies can be found.
 

What kinds of resources will be needed to fully implement and scale your concept?

A brand expert would be needed to create a strong label. Then partnerships with pilot companies would be needed. As well as a set up of initial marketing for the label.
