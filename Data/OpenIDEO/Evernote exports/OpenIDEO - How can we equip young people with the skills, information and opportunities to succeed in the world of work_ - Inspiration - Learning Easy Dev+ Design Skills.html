Learning Easy Dev+ Design Skills

Young workers can easily learn how to build simple websites/mobile apps and related design/coding/assembly skills. There are many open-source tools that are easily to learn and quick to use with a vast pool of online support available for learners.
Mobile apps have become very easy to build and many businesses are eager to have them, no matter how simple.  This is a growing industry that will continue to evolve within the next 10 years.  Building skills in an evolving industry with which they have everyday touch can give a more sustainable mode of living/learning/income.  Moreover, youth all over the world are using more mobile apps and there are many unmet local needs that can be addressed.  Also, both US and UK/EU governments are making more data available, a trend that is likely to continue.
