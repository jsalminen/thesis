E Waste collection System

Apart from thinking how we can recycle the E waste, we must also think upon how to collect it from the society, In order to minimize the dumping of the E waste.
E Waste Collection System
Hi All,
Here my prime focus is in how to be able to collect from the society, the E waste. Generally, as I have felt, people doesn't even know

what are the harms of E Waste?
where to give away their "once prized possessions"?
and what if the data gets re produced?

I have seen many people around me with huge pile ups of CDs, DVDs in their homes which are useless to them, but still they prefer to keep them. With new type of technology getting introduced older gets obsolete, and then we either keep it up in a shelf(if the size is small) or we dump it out on the streets.

My Concept focus on setting up Government Authorized Electronic Waste Collection centers, authorized to collect E Wastes. These Centers shall work in their allotted areas where they will be responsible for the Mass Awareness Activities, and collecting E waste, along with proper segregation of the waste material for proper disposal to respective recycling unit.

These centers should be equipped with memory shredder, with which, when they collect any such memory based equipment, the contributor is satisfied that whatever data it possessed won't be reproduced.
for eg. I have a crashed hard drive, which is of no use to me, but it had my personal data, now If I think of surrendering at such a center, I will think of any possibility that the data will not be reproduced.

Such centers, along with mass awareness, we shall strive towards making this earth a more greener place to live in.

How does your concept safeguard human health and protect our environment?

By providing a platform to each individual who have ever used any electronic equipment, to be able to contribute towards E Waste recycling. By educating the society about the harmful effects of the E wastes to the environment.

Where does your concept fit into the lifecycle of electronic devices?

My concept will explore the untapped potential of E Waste Management Industry. By changing the existing model of B2B to B2C.

What steps could be taken today to start implementing your concept?

To start with a one pilot Center for a Specific Area. This small Center will be the collection center for E waste, Contributors will be attracted by bill boards, advertisements, etc.

Local Authorities could be contacted to authorizes the center and help.
Likewise, studying the trend and difficulties, It could be stretched to a bigger level.

What kinds of resources will be needed to fully implement and scale your concept?

Educating Masses is a Lengthy process and shall be done properly and effectively with proper Governmental assistance.
