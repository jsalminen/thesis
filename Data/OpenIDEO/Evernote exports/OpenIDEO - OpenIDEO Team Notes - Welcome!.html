Aug 2nd 2010 Welcome!

 

Welcome to field notes! 1000's of post-its, wireframes, prototypes and pixels later we're in Beta but hopefully the journey's just beginning.



We thought that it would be fun to have a place on the OpenIDEO platform that allows us to share stuff that's been generated through the course of challenges, general IDEO content and just as importantly inspirational things from the wider world. Feel free to contact us at fieldnotes@openideo.com if you have any suggested content to feature.
