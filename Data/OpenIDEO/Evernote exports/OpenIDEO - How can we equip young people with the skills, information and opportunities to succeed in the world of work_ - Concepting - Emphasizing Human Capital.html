Emphasizing Human Capital

In a world where job opportunities can't keep up with a booming population, we need a systems approach: turn products into services (e.g. the shared economy) and emphasize human capital and human power over machine power.
Image source: http://crenshawsubway.org/2012/04/the-peoples-motion/peoplepower/
I think we should emphasize providing more opportunities in addition to providing more skills and information. Countries such as India and China might not have all the modern infrastructure, but what they do have is a lot of people. Economies could capitalize on their people power to add value where machines can't, for example providing personalized services or using the idea of the "shared economy" to turn products into services (thus also increasing access to limited resources/products).
