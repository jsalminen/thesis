Apr 2nd 2012 A Collaborative Journey + U

 

 



The +U volunteering mobile app is the result of the Open Planet Ideas challenge where ideas were sought to create sustainable lifestyles from existing technologies. Open Planet Ideas is powered by OpenIDEO so includes many similar collaborative features that folks enjoy on our site. The +U app has launched in the UK Android Marketplace and it's open source code has been released so that organisations can adapt it to fit their needs to create even more positive impact. And +U supports our mantra that we're better, together!
