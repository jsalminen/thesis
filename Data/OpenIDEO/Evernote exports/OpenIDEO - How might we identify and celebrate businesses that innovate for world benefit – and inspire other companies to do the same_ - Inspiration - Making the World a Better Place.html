Making the World a Better Place

Some solutions which i believe would help to increase wealth and prosperity of people all around the world and to stop exploitation of people.
Even though it sounds like a impossible task to do; to create a world where everyone has quality food, affordable housing and education, top-tier medical care, ubiquitous clean energy, dignified opportunity, thriving economies, and global peace and security. There might be some methods to achieve this utopia.

First of all, i think this goal starts by politicians rather than business organizations, since politicians and politics are the enforcer about business, markets and economics and at the same time they are the people which have to take care about their people, therefor this goal can be reached by their guidance. All of the companies and business in todays global world are profit organizations, the non-profit ones are not counted as business but as organizations which try to help. This makes people not so eager to help since they don't earn anything from them. Thats why there are not so much non-profit organization or at least not enough to help all the people around the world.

In my opinion, to motivate business which works for profit to invest on helping people, all governments around to world should make a contract and follow the rules on that contract to make their people wealthier and to increase prosperity.This contract could be like this: all of the big business organizations in a country could invest on for example making house for homeless and poor people rather than bidding the most in a auction by the government, so the organization which provides most houses for the poor people could win the auction (this could also remove the corruption by the governments since the money would directly go for poor people, the government won't touch the money). This method can be used for big companies as i said , because not all the business in a country can afford to enter a auction but since big companies need these kind of auctions to increase their growth something like this can be applied to them.

In case of other or small business' in a country, they could sponsor poor families or like sponsor non-profit organizations in that country rather then paying tax to government. For example rather then paying the tax % for a deal they do, they can take care of a persons who's in need of money and who's sick and can't afford it's hospital money. These are just examples but what i'm trying to say is that every business in a country could do this humanitarian things proportionally with what they earn and profit. If they are not a very rich company they could buy food for people in need instead of giving tax and so on.

As a conclusion, even though my thoughts can be seen unrealistic, i believe to achieve this goal the governments play a key role because of the reasons i mentioned above. So in order to make things like this every government should follow that contract first of all and support these kind of innovations because i believe if they do this rather then stealing from their people and doing corruption , they could reach the utter goal of a government: to make their people wealthy and prosper.
