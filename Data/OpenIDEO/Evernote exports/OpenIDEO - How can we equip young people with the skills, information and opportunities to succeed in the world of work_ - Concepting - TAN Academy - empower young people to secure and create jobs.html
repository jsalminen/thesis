TAN Academy - empower young people to secure and create jobs

TAN Academy is a platform that provides Training, Advising, and Networking opportunities to help young people discover their career interests, develop new skills, and succeed in the world of work.

Equipping young people with skills, information, and opportunities to succeed in the world of work requires a platform that targets and serves the needs of young people in different circumstances.



Instead of categorizing young people by their interests, age groups, education background, or financial capabilities and cater to these categories, TAN Academy divides young people into three groups according to their needs, and helps them succeed by providing them with skills, information, and opportunities.


Three groups are:
1: Those who know what they would like to pursue but lack essential or additional skills.
2: Those who are lost and don't know what career path to pursue.
3: Those who would like to know what others are going through (job hunting, workplace transition, career development, etc.) and/or contribute their own experiences.


To empower these groups, TAN Academy provides the following assistances both online and at local TAN Academy offices.:


1: Training - Helps young people to find appropriate training resources so they can get the job they want. It could be a computer programing certificate, a degree from the local community college, or simply some public speaking lessons. Local TAN Academy offices also offer training classes that meet the demands of local economies and industries.


2: Advising - Provides career advising to young people and direct them to critical information to help them find and develop their career interests. Local offices offer workshops hosted by young professionals and arrange tours to local companies.


3: Networking - Provides an online platform for young people to share information and talk about their challenges and experiences. This allows young people to compare their experiences and communicate and learn from each other. Meetings and outings are arranged by local offices to facilite in-person networking, exchange of job opportunities, and friendship building.

How will your concept support young people as they transition into the world of work?

The TAN Academy is build not only to help and prepare young people to find jobs, but also support them as they transition into the world of work. Using the networking platform, they can talk about their challenges and share their experiences online and build important contacts. When their career path becomes uncertain, they can schedule an appointment with a TAN Academy advisor to discuss their issues and gain some insights. When they feel they need extra skills to move up their career ladder, they can find resources to receive the required training.

What online or in-person components of your concept will best support this transition?

The entire TAN Academy platform, through its online and in-person components, will support young people's transition into the world of work. TAN Academy hopes that those who received help and found it helpful would choose to give back by becoming a career advisor and by offering training classes with TAN Academy. The easiest way to contribute is by actively participating in TAN Academy's online forums.
