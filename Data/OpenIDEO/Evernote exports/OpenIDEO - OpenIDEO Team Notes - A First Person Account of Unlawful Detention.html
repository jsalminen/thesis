Oct 31st 2011 A First Person Account of Unlawful Detention

 

We understand that our Amnesty Challenge presents situations and stories that are outside many of our own day-to-day experiences. To gain empathy and better understand what it's like to have a loved one detained unlawfully, we would like to introduce you to Amina. Her husband disappeared in July of 2005 and in this video she speaks about the emotional anguish that it has caused her and her family.








Come join us to create new technology ideas that support family members like Amina and those working to uphold human rights in the face of unlawful detention.
