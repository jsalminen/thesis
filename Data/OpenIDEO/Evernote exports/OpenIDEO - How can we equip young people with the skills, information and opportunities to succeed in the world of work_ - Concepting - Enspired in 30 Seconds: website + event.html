Enspired in 30 Seconds: website + event

Online story sampler: collection of 30sec videos profiling people with cool jobs: what do you do? how did you get there? + 'speed dating' style event to put young people in touch with same.
 
quick sketch from OpenStorm process
Problem
When I was a student, I had no idea that there could be such a thing as an awesome job. Thankfully, I now have an awesome job. 5 years ago there was no name for my job. I could never have predicted or planned for getting this job, but I could tell you I how got here. I think this is a common experience for lots of the most inspiring jobs, the ones that can actually get young people excited about work.


Solution 
Inform young people about what kind of awesome inspiring jobs others are doing, why they are rewarding/inspiring/fulfilling and how they got there. 


Website
A collection of 30 second videos of lots of different people talking about their jobs, following the basic formula: 


What: I do ____ for a living. 
Why: It is fulfilling because _____. 
How: I got here by ____ + _____ + ...


Event
The website would be supported by 'speed dating' style events where young people get to spend 5 minutes each with 12 interesting people.


p.s. I misspelled "Enspired" in the title intentionally  because I work as part of a network of social entrepreneurs called Enspiral
 
 

My Virtual Team

Sam Rye, Alanna Krause, Nanz Nair (and Meena from the OpenIDEO team) who all workshopped this idea with me at an OpenStorm workshop at Enspiral HQ in Wellington, New Zealand today.
