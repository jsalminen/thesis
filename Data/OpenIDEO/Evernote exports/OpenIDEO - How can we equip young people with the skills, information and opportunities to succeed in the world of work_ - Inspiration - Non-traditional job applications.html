Non-traditional job applications

Matthew Epstein: video resume and marketing campaign to get the attention of Google
 
Google Video Resume - Matthew Epstein
With so many job applicants, it can be useful to find a new way to differentiate yourself from the crowd. 


In this example, Matthew Epstein created a viral video and website -http://googlepleasehire.me/ - aiming to demonstrate his marketing abilities, with the goal of getting hired by Google. 


This approach isn't without risk - in the end he didn't receive an offer from Google. However, he did receive job offers from several startups, and the video has racked up over 1 million views on YouTube.
