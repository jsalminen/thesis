May 4th 2011
OpenIDEO Currents 22


We're still reeling from the news that OpenIDEO scooped a prestigious Webby Award in the Community section – up against the likes of Twitter and Tumblr! Our congrats go out to you all – you're what makes us Community. Aptly tweeted by OpenIDEATOR Arjan Tupan: Can I say this just once: *we* rock!

As the Local Food Challenge charges through it's Applause Phase, it's concepts have been attracting high-fives and fresh commentary. Next week we'll be announcing a shortlist of OpenIDEATORS who will be asked to refine their concepts further with the help of our community. Let's do Queensland proud with our collective, creative intelligence on aligning food production and food consumption!

And ever wondered what song would best sum up OpenIDEO? We asked via Twitter and Facebook and posted your thoughts on our new OpenEXPRESS Forum. Expect more questions coming your way from there soon and get behind the buzz!
