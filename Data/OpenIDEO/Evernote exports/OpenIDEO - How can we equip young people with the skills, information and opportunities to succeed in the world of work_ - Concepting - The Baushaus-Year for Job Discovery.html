The Baushaus-Year for Job Discovery

Learning from the Bauhaus, let's give young people the opportunity to explore different jobs, industries, and career paths in one condensed into one year.
 
Bauhaus-ish logo
One of the revolutionary ideas in the Bauhaus was the common first year: Students excepted into the prestigious school spent their first year studying all disciplines the school was teaching, before they were allowed to choose their specific field of study.


Perhaps we could learn from the Bauhaus and create a Bauhaus-Year for jobs?


Young people graduating from high-school could sign up for the Bauhaus year in their home town, or any other city of their choice (or where there are Bauhaus years on offer) and for one year they will have a total of 3 internships. Each at a different company for the duration of 4 months (with one week holiday between each internship).


 
 

How will your concept support young people as they transition into the world of work?

It will allow them to explore different careers and opportunities in one intense year.
 

What online or in-person components of your concept will best support this transition?

It will need a platform that helps so organize the Bauhaus Year and brings the young people and the companies together.
