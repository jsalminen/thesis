TryMe

Most times, entering the market is difficult because you have no chance to properly demonstrate your skills. The only thing people go after is your CV, and that does not do you justice, or show who you truly are.
A case film showing just the beginning of what a platform like this could achieve.
By creating a new friction-less system for the job market, we give employers the chance to seek for competence without the fluff. We aim to bridge the gap between employers and job seekers.
