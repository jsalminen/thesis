Toolbox

A online area where skills like Powerpoint, Excel, and any others that we can think of are taught in various forms. Print versions, Videos, Interactive do as you go lessons. It would really help fill the gaps in higher education.
 
The Toolbox
I see a website where anyone can go to and learn any work-related skill they want. They can become Wizards of Powerpoint, Excel, Business Intelligence Systems, or whatever else we can think of. It's tough to go into a new environment and have to learn on the job. So an aggregation of all of the tools that a young professional in one place would be very powerful. It wouldn't just be for young people though. Anyone could use it. The project can have a nation even worldwide impact if it is given the right direction. If you keep things simple and organize them by industry this could go far. Eg for Finance: Excel, Powerpoint, Oracle, SAP etc. Provide sample exercises for people to do, related to the field. They can then see if it might be something they enjoy/ are interested in.
 
 

How will your concept support young people as they transition into the world of work?

It will fill in any skill gaps so they can approach their first day/week/month with greater confidence. It will give them a "test drive" so they are able to find something that suits them potentially. They can list these skills on their resumes which is a way recruiters find people for entry level jobs. At the end of the day it gives them tools to have in their back pocket so they can feel confident about making a great impression because of the skills that they have been give.
 

What online or in-person components of your concept will best support this transition?

This is a share based website. I do not have much technical knowledge but something like a Wikipedia model would be plausible in my mind. Just an open-source area where people can find information they need that is simple and easily digestible.
