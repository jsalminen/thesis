Mission #1 Understand E-Waste
Let’s collect everything we can to learn about what e-waste is and how it presents an opportunity for innovation in communities around the world:

  • What goes into making our favorite cell phone, laptop or other electronic tool? 
  • How does e-waste recycling work today? 
  • What happens when our e-waste isn’t disposed of or recycled properly?

Let’s also explore examples of existing or successful e-waste innovations, services or businesses to learn from. What's working? Who can we learn from? Think reuse, repurpose, secondary markets, safe recycling, etc.
