Get ready to make some noise! This week on the Web Start-up Challenge we switch from the ideational goodness of the Concepting phase to the Applause phase. This gives folks time look over all the ideas which have been posted & hit the applaud button on their favourites to help our challenge team devise a shortlist of concepts. Keep up those conversations too – they'll fuel fresh thinking as we approach the collaborative Refinement phase.


We're excited at the ITIF announcement of grant funding to improve election accessibility. This provides an opportunity for collaboration to take ideas from our Voting Challenge forward. As always, we're keen on transformation of ideas into impact, so we are enthusiastic about possibilities arising from this call for proposals.


Given the global spread of our OpenIDEO community, we know it's not spring everywhere – but think everyone will appreciate a dose of fresh thinking from the IDEO design community. Check out Spring Fever!
