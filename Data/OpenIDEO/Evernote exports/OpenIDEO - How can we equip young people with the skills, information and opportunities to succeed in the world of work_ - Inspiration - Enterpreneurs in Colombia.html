Enterpreneurs in Colombia

In my country there are many initiatives that support young entrepreneurs, this is one of them.
 
Web page
 
"The Colombian Ministry of Culture and the Corporation Center for Science and Technology of Antioquia-CTA (Entity promoting agendas,mechanisms of action and projects that build Relationships effective way knowledge flows between the public,research,education and business, to convert the knowledge, science and technology in dynamic factors for progress economic and social development).


C3 + d:  Creating  Culture and  Digital Content  Lab  is a venture  aimed at  digital  content creators with  cultural aproach , with  a project  or business idea  viable, robust  and relevant . Not all content creators  interested in entering the  C3 + d  should  have ideas  for profit, but should seek economic sustainability of their projects.

In  its second version, this laboratory, organized by the Ministry of Culture and the Centre for Science and Technology o fAntioquia (CTA),seeks to streng then the administrative and managerial capacitiesof the participants, through training, exchange of experiences with other creators, reflection on relevant business models for digital content sector and business management to realizes pecific generation of cultural content in digital environments." (Translated from spanish).
