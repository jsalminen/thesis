Creative juices continue to flow on the Amnesty Challenge this week – with Concepts being posted, discussed and built upon to address human rights. It's been great to see collaborators adding illustrations such as Margaret Hagan on the Human Rights Graphic Narrative Platform, video as in Nayda Florez Venizelo's The Connectedness of Co-working and even prototypes, like by our very own Nathan Waterhouse, with his Local Advice Wiki. It's a rich mix of ideas which we're hoping you'll be adding to before the Concepting phase closes next week.


To help to gain insights into issues surrounding unlawful detention, we posted this clip with the personal story from a wife of a detainee. Supporting the Supporters is a key theme of our search for tech solutions on this challenge, so we hope this video inspires fresh thoughts.
