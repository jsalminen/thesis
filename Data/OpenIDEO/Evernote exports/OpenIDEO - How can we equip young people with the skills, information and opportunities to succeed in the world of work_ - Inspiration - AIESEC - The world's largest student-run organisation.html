AIESEC - The world's largest student-run organisation

For over 60 years, students from around the World have organized themselves to develop their leadership potential by taking roles on local/international community projects and international internships. Currently with +60K members!
 
AIESEC Intro for students
 
This is a global organization run by students for students. 


Their main aim is to provide opportunities to young people to explore and develop their potential and become agents of positive change.


So, it is about gaining skills, developing leadership competencies, working with people from different cultures and learning about the key global issues.


And they do this by partnering with key players of the society: NGOs, SMEs, Multinationals, Schools, Universities, Local Governments; while maintaining full independence on the way the organization is managed.


The students joining the program enter a very complete learning journey:


  • Access to a global platform to share knowledge and network.

  • Access to training and workshops on hard management skills as well as personal development (over 470 conferences going on globally per year).

  • Opportunity to do an international internship (length, background and key skills depend on what it is available and what the student can offer and want to learn).

  • Opportunity to join and lead local/international teams that focus on key local issues as well as general organizational management (+20K leadership roles per year).

  • Access to an Alumni Network of almost 1M individuals and 4K of Partners around the world.

  • Career Events for students to connect with potential employers from all sectors.
