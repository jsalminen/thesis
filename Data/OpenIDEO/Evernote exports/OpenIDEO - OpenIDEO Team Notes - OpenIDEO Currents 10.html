We're amped to report that we've crossed 150 inspirations on the Maternal Health + Mobile Technology Challenge! Come join us and add to the current collection covering business models, popular culture, inclusivity, connectivity, communications and more. Your comments are as valuable as your posts – let's have a conversation! We've even had the likes of Ken Banks from Frontline SMS dropping by to provide generous advice and support.

San Francisco based OpenIDEATOR Anjelika Deogirikar posted a brilliant account of the OpenIDEO SF tweet-up over on the Support Forums. Inspiring indeed for those of you who might be thinking to host a tweet-up of your own. Remember you can subscribe to forums by email – by clicking the envelope icon on the specific forum – to stay up to date on conversations and join in to post, vote and comment there.

We're also busy chipping away on upcoming new features for OpenIDEO – part of our always-in-beta launch-and-learn approach. This week we started highlighting a daily Featured Inspiration on our current challenge. And we continue to feature posts on Facebook and Twitter... because there's so much inspiration from the community to share!
