Last week, our buddies at IDEO.org posted this exciting update from on the ground in Ghana. A little over a year ago, Unilever, Water and Sanitation for the Urban Poor (WSUP) and IDEO partnered to explore different low-cost sanitation options in Ghana. OpenIDEO was part of the picture via our Sanitation Challenge. In this second phase of the project, IDEO.org is tasked with building out the brand strategy of the Clean Team toilet service and are continuing to explore and prototype solutions based on local user insights.
