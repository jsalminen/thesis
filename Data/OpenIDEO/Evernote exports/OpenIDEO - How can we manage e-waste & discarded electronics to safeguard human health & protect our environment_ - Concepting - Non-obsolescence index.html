Non-obsolescence index

Creation an index that measures the obsolenscence taxes about the products and give tax benefits to the companies that shows better durabilitty in their products.
That concept was generated by undergraduate students in a workshop we promoted
It's all about changing the way companies view profits sources. Instead of offering new products often, they can offer durability. The government would help providing tax benefits to the companies that prove that their products have a specific amount of durability.
With tax benefits the company is able to investment more money, reflecting as a consequence in its profits. It is illustrated in the graphic.

How does your concept safeguard human health and protect our environment?

It avoids e-waste creation.

Where does your concept fit into the lifecycle of electronic devices?

With the government help, the manufactures are stimulated to design long lasting products.

What steps could be taken today to start implementing your concept?

Campaigns to expose the problem, mobilizing the community.

What kinds of resources will be needed to fully implement and scale your concept?

Manufacturers and government must work together to achieve this. Their goals must be aligned.
