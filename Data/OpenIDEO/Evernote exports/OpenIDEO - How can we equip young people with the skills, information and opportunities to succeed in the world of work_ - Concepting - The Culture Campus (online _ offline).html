The Culture Campus (online / offline)

The concept is about developing an online platform besides a culture campus where any career-level can meet, learn or coach across disciplines. Where creative learning is a playful give and take, from employees to job-seekers AND vice versa!
Creative Learning Spiral

Learning today shouldn't mean just to pass on know-how rather more it should be a chance to combine two things, knowledge and creativity, to innovate and create new things. And you learn/teach people best if you let them play or share a passion.

So (hopefully) everyone have a life besides business. So there may be one passion that engraves you and enriches your skills. Skills, that may be totally aside your profession - a passion like cooking, gardening, coding or skating, etc. Combine them with your job/profession. This skill could bring new, fresh energy to a team/ company.

Especially absurd/foreign disciplines like professional skating, acting, cooking or gardening may make no sense to you at first sight but “...on the roof of the Google offices in London there’s a garden where tomatoes and sunflowers are grown. Dive in and do a bit of weeding if you fancy it.”

Let (external) mentors tell their story of passion and let them inspire you/anyone how is interested and open-minded. (Pls. watch the awesome Rodney Mullen at the TEDxUSC - How Context Shapes Content).

If you would ask young graduates/job-seekers to teach your employees/ company, you not just value their usual business input, further more you see the person as a whole, and take him serious as that.

Therefore platform and the culture campus should be open for everyone. It also should enhance awareness of creativity and support a playful and meaningful interactivity besides the relevant business disciplines. You will create an atmosphere of identity not uniformity, an atmosphere of appreciation and respect not just efficiency. 

If employer, employees and job seekers could meet this way, a lot more young graduates could have a chance, without the perfect reference/qualification; because of a cultural-added-value. 

Job seekers will be rational and emotional committed. Employees will be motivated to achieve even more for the company and for their career just because it’s fun to work, to learn and to teach, and equally to benefit from everything. Additionally companies could enrich their business culture. (Hint: Employer Branding)

Issues could be: motivation, inspiration, social, absurd, fun… but also more concrete - coding, financing, definitely free courses on design thinking ;-)

Contentcould come as: web blogs, images, storytelling, video performance/ games, and workshops, mentoring one-on-one…
