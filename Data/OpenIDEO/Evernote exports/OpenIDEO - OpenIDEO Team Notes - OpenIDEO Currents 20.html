Drums are rolling here at OpenIDEO! We've recently announced the winners of our Bone Marrow Donation Challenge – trumpeting in some high-notes from our collaborative orchestrata. Heart-felt thanks to *all* of you who participated in this challenge and sung in the choir against cancer.

The Local Food Challenge continues to be blowing our minds with its canny, colourful and comprehensive concepts. Folks are posting a fab range of concepts including food trucks, phone apps and foraging events – with some savvy business and distribution models thrown in for good measure. One week to go guys – get your ideas posted to help us re-imagine the future of food. You might even win the chance to join us in sunny Queensland at the IDEAS 2011 Festival. And check out IDEO's Paul Bennett talking about the challenge and his vinyl record obsession.

Only a few days left to get your evaluative goodness around our Maternal Health + Mobile Technology Challenge. OpenIDEATORS have worked long, hard and in unison to compose their concepts. Join us to help decide which ideas are going to best effect positive change.

There's well over 14 000 of you these days. Let's make some noise!
