Community Champion Shares Clues on Connecting


We love it when OpenIDEATORS pitch in to help each other out. It speaks to our conviction that OpenIDEO is a place where we can design better, together. So we were particularly heartened over the weekend when prolific-poster Arjan Tupan featured this gem of a clip which he put together and featured over on our Community: Helping Each Other Out User Forum.

I noticed that with every new challenge, new people join OpenIDEO. That is great. Also, I noticed that it takes some time for new joiners to get used to all the functionality the platform has to offer. One function that is really great is connecting contributions. To make that easy for new joiners, I have made a short How-to video. – Arjan Tupan

Cheers Arjan! Fab community spirit and we look forward to more action from OpenIDEATORS supporting each other with collaborative goodness.
