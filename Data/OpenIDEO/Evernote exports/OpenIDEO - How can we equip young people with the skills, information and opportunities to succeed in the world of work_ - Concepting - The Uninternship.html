The Uninternship

Job sampling through an online library of reality video profiles of actual job roles.
Personal and honest portrayal of job roles by the mentors themselves

Job sampling used to require an internship or several to get a real flavour of a profession, career or job role. Organising an internship requires a lot of resources and is limited by availability. How many internships can a young person complete before they have to make decisions on a career path? Imagine if the insights provided by an internship that aid career choices can be compressed into a 5 minute video. Mentors and volunteers will create and upload videos of their working lives. These videos will contain the highlights and business as usual parts of their job roles as well as capture the working environment whether it is in an office, laboratory, studio or outdoors. Young people can browse through these videos at their pleasure and appreciate hundreds of diverse job roles. Their curiosity may lead them to post a question or leave a comment.

This cannot replace all the experiences gained by doing an internship but aims to provide a short-cut to the ones that contribute to making career choices. A single video can be viewed by thousands of young people whereas an internship can only serve one.

How will your concept support young people as they transition into the world of work?

If they find something they like or are inspired by they are likely to pursue it with passion.
