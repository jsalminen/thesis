The collective-work-pedia which erradicates miopia

An Internet-based platform that allows for different actors (Schools, Companies, Entrepreneurs, Job seekers, ...) to interact to build knowledge that is useful for each type of actors and provides 'eye-glasses' to the young to move ahead on their way
Collective knowledge acts as eye-glasses, allowing for taking a next move
The workforce activity is in constant change and creates a knowledge that is today required to make decisions and that is only in the mind of those in a particular work fish-tank. The collective-work-pedia is a platform that allows for the different actors to BE PROACTIVE and build KNOWLEDGE PEER to PEER. Information is a powerful tool that allows everyone to move ahead, a powerful tool that breaks fish-tanks in the benefit of everyone. For instance:
- JOB SEEKERS: 1) Access to viewpoints of professionals that are currently in a workplace they would like to have ... what their career path has been ... what their next move can be ...Â  2) Access to professionals with similar passions 2) Many new positions created in the workspace that many students do not even have the chance to know what it means; companies or professionals could collaborate collectively publishing what the positions are, what the skills are, how to gain skills etc
- ENTREPRENEURS: I have come across entrepreneurs that contacted me to help them 'speak the same language of a VC' to raise funds and move forward, or I have seen entrepreneurs exchange their experiences and learning from one another
- RECRUITERS: Many recruiters could get valuable collective information on skills that are not being detected in their methods and that are key for performing in certain positions; or skills which are wrongly demanded in a particular position
- SCHOOLS: 1) Many schools could get collective information to better understand their existing gaps in programs (i.e basic knowledge on entrepreneurship) 2) New schools could emerge out information on unemployment rates, profiles, nationalities, etc which could help erradicate the gap
- VCs: Could collectively provide information to potential entrepreneurs (I have seen many oral presentations with great information ... common mistakes and son on ... that is by the nature of the process restricted to an audience. Why not make it available only-one-click-away. An on the other hand, VCs could learn from entrepreneurs what their stand points are ... I have also seen VCs change the way they decide on an investment based on their past experience ... why not share this knowledge on behalf of others?

How will your concept support young people as they transition into the world of work?

Young people in transition need to understand the context in which they have to shape their way. If they do not understand it, the jungle becomes hostile, difficult.
