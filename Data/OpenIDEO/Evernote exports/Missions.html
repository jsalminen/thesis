MISSION #2Skills & Training
As new sectors and technologies emerge, there’s also a need to develop skills beyond those traditionally taught within formal education and training. What are some skills – soft and hard – you have encountered that you think are important for young people to acquire in order to succeed in the workplace?

New training and learning models are also emerging that help people to acquire and sharpen their skills outside of their formal education. We’d love you to share any examples you’ve seen of new and inspiring ways to develop soft or hard skills that are happening beyond the classroom.


MISSION #1Hurdles & Success Stories
Personal experiences can serve as the best kind of inspiration for new ideas. We’d love to hear your story of transitioning from education to employment but also stories about other big life changes you’ve been through. So whether it was leaving school and starting work, leaving home, moving abroad or starting a family, tell us what was daunting, what was exciting, who helped you prepare, what actually happened and what you learned from it all?

You could even venture further and interview someone you know about their experience of a big life change. 

MISSION #3The Employer’s Perspective
We would love to hear from employers and businesses about your perspective on the challenge of equipping young people for work. 

What have been some of your own hurdles and successes in working with young people? What challenges do you face in recruiting and training young people in your business? What support have you tapped into or felt lacking?

MISSION #4Surprise Us
What have we missed? Inspire us with insights, examples and stories which go beyond our other missions for this challenge.

Is there something you’ve seen out there that might inspire fresh thinking about this challenge? Share it with us, whatever form it may take. It doesn’t matter where it’s come from - as long as it’s relevant to the question of supporting young people as they transition from education into employment we want to know about it! 



MISSION #5Collaborative Partnerships
Various organisations from different sectors coming together can generate a far greater impact than any one of them could have achieved individually. Please share examples of such partnerships that broaden opportunities and aspirations for young people.

What can be done to increase the involvement and interaction between potential partners?  How might businesses, governments educators and NGOs better collaborate?  We welcome all thoughts on how to encourage more of these partnerships and guarantee their success.
