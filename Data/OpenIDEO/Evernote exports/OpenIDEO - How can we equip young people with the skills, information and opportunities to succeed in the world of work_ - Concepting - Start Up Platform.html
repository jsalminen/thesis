Start Up Platform

Bring together Start Up Entrepreneurs, financiers and young talented people in order to find a future job, an employee or financing.
 
 

What is a Start UP platform?

Inspired by the NYC Startup Jobfair http://nycstartupjobfair.com/about/ and Ana Cecilias "Young + Unemployd Conference" I like the idea of bringing 

  • Start up entrepreneurs
  • Investors like business angles and venture capitalists
  • Young talented people looking for a job

together.

On this network event people can meet in coffee corners, can present them-selfs and/or their business in an exhibition space and can pitch their start ups (as startup entrepreneur) as well as their personal skills (as young talent).



What`s in for me?

Start up entrepreneurs can 

  1. pitch their idea to future investors
  2. present their company to potential customers
  3. find new employees for their growing business



Investors can

 

 

 

  1. scout potential start ups for investment
  2. find new employees to join their company or one of the start ups they invested in

 

Young talents can

 

  1. pitch their skills to find a future employer
  2. find out about a jobs in a sector beyond large corporations and civil service
  3. find potential employers
  4. get inspired by other start ups to create an own business



 

 

 





 
 

How will your concept support young people as they transition into the world of work?

They get to know opportunities in the start up and investment sector. Young talents have the chance to present them-selfs as well as to get to know other inspiring people during the networking event. Additionally they have the opportunity to find a job or to create one.
 

What online or in-person components of your concept will best support this transition?

An organizational team is needed to organize the administration and to find the right start ups, investors and talents to join. Additionally facilities for the network event are needed.
