Guiding Principles for Our Business Impact Challenge



Our Business Impact Challenge tackles an important issue – one that we believe offers a lot of opportunity for learning, discussion and impact-focused solutions. Still, the topic may feel overwhelming and complex to many of us learning about it for the first time. With that in mind we thought we'd take a minute to outline some Guiding Principles for our focus and our conversation together.


Our Focus

If we think about our challenge question, and what it's really asking, there are a few key points that we want to emphasise:

Business as a Force for Positive Impact

The role that business can and should play in our global society is one that has been heatedly discussed and debated, particularly over the last few years. While not every company can or should be celebrated as a responsible or sustainable corporate citizen, our intention in this challenge is to approach the business sector with a sense of optimism, hope and potential: how might companies play a role in shaping our future for the better? What companies are already leading the way? So, rather than focusing on what's not working, or doubting the positive impact that the for-profit sector might have, let's think and dream big to design ways that business can lead our world in a positive direction.

What Do We Mean By 'Business'?

And speaking of business, how are we defining 'business' in this challenge? For our efforts together, we're talking about any organisation that makes a profit – this could be a global, mainstream corporation, a local enterprise or something in between. core operations rather than a charitable add-on or donation strategy, we're interested in it. 

Conversely, our conversation together is not meant to focus on nonprofits, corporate foundations, private foundations or other entities that do not earn a profit or ones that view 'doing good' as only a charitable add-on or donation strategy. 

World Benefit

The term world benefit can feel big, broad and maybe even confusing – how do we know what it is? How do we know once we've achieved it? For our purposes in this challenge, we're leaving it up to our community to shape the definition of world benefit: what does it mean and look like to you? Jump right in during our Inspiration phase and share your thoughts with us.

Inspiring and Empowering Other Businesses to Follow Suit

This challenge isn't just about uncovering examples of businesses that operate with world benefit in mind; it's about inspiring and empowering other companies to join in this movement toward a more flourishing future. Much like a roadmap that shows a traveler the way forward, we're approaching this topic as a journey that takes time, resources and commitment on the part of the companies involved. How might we help chart their course forward? As you dive into our Inspiration phase and share your ideas in Concepting, we encourage you to keep this in mind.


Our Conversation

Our global community comprises an eclectic mix of experiences, opinions, perspectives and approaches – and there's real value in this diversity. As we collaborate with community members from around the world, let's always remember to keep our tone positive, our outlook optimistic and our dialogue respectful.
