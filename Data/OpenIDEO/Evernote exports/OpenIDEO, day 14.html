Analysis
  • Trying to make a framework of the rules with post-it notes. Data used does not include comments; mostly blogs, challenge briefs and official instructions + some instructions by users on the forum, and my own reflections on the diary. Idea is to make a framework of what categories of rules there might be. I believe I have sufficient amount of data (about 300 web clips) to form the categories, even if I haven't got all the rules yet. The specific rules I found later, for example from comments, can then be used to expand and validate the initial framework.
  • Note: Legal rules is one important class of rules, but I left it out of the post-it analysis, as the legal rules are explicitly stated on the site and easy to find.  
  • Rules were gathered from the data as follows:
    1. Code the data
    2. Extract excerpts with code "Rules". Rule was understood broadly, including both explicit (guiding principles) and implicit rules ("We're a chatty bunch at OpenIDEO)
    3. Print excerpts
    4. Read trough and underline the rules
    5. Write the essence of rules to post-it notes
    6. Organize notes
    7. Name emergent categories
