design for disassembly? laser printer...

time laps video of the tedious and long process of disassembling an old laser printer. .
printer tear down HD
towards the end of the video you see the stacks of seperated matrials: PS, some ABS and tin plate but also quite some nasty composite/e-waste - circuit boards, wires, motors etc that are near to impossible to separate for subsequent recycling.

the ingeneers did an amazing job to make that thing compact and easy to assemble. i'm sure they would come up with similar clever solutions to make the products easy to disassemble, if only their boss would ask them to do so. 
why would he do so? maybee because recycling is cheaper for some reason: due to laws and taxes (unlikely - strong lobby) or due to raising material prices (peak oil?) ...but i'm getting to far.
