LAB 21: Co-Learning Labs with 21st Century Guidance Counselors

LAB 21 is an integrative co-learning space ushering young people into the 21st century.
Studio/Cafe/Creative Business by Ant Clausen Photography

There are quite a few great ideas on OpenIdeo about bridging the skills, information and opportunity gaps to equip young people for success in the future.

But where do young people gather 'offline' to do so over and over again?

Where do 'they' enter the picture, first and foremost?

Housed in one building, LAB 21 would bridge media and tech stations with modern elements of a library, cafe and event space, thereby reconciling a dedicated 'go-to' place for young people to communally build skills, gather information and cease opportunities to carve new career paths.

LAB 21 would be managed as a social enterprise and directed by a diverse and multi-disciplinary team of leaders and lab stewards who design and create programs, events, conferences, internships, co-ops, etc that essentially meet the mission of this OpenIdeo Challenge.

Creativity and Design Thinking would be inherent in the various LAB 21 curricula.

Individuals, parents, educators, secondary schools, universities, governments, non-profits, foundations, businesses, etc, in service to young people can be engaged in a manner suitable to the lab's mission.

Based on student interests, businesses and/or brands that are socially motivated may choose to sponsor LAB 21, a student-member, or a group of members.

Similar to a library, student-members would need a LAB 21 I.D Card to gain entry into the space and to track their budding career progress.

LAB 21 can be as pervasive as an Apple store or as ubiquitous as Starbucks, each tailored to the specific needs of the communities served.

How will your concept support young people as they transition into the world of work?

Apart from providing a stationary, 'offline', collaborative learning environment with direct access to viable people and resources, LAB 21 alumni will be required to share their stories, career trajectories and perform mentorship duties to new and incoming members.

Additionally, every space would replicate a work/office environment conducive to work/life balance.

LAB 21 (or something similar) has an opportunity to become a reliable, household name for young people envisioning the future and actively seeking information, skills and opportunities for tomorrow's jobs.
