Repair Cafes

A NYTimes article highlights a new trend called Repair Cafe.
http://www.nytimes.com/imagepages/2012/05/09/world/AMSTERDAM-2.html
I came across this article recently and just knew I needed to share it here.

What happens when a bunch of people – committed to reusing what they have and excited about getting their hands dirty – decide to repair rather than buy new? It sounds like these Repair Cafes have started in The Netherlands, but I actually got an email from someone looking to start one in the Bay Area too!

On OpenIDEO we've talked a lot about bartering or trading services (especially in our Vibrant Cities Challenge), but this I think is a fun, new take on community togetherness, consumption and recycling. 

You can check out the full article here.
