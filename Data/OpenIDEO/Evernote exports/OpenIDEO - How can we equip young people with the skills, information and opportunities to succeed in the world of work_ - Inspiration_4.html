Job Sampling & Internships

It’s tough to truly understand what it’s like to work at a company or in an industry without giving it a go. In the Inspiration phase we heard that internships and mentoring filled this need for some, but that they weren’t easy to find and were often poorly managed. Could we reinvent internships? Are there were other creative ways to try roles or experiencing different decisions?

With this theme we seek new concepts to help young people try different roles and industries, whether it be through internships, work experience or completely new approaches.

How might we help young people try multiple roles to make smarter decisions?
How might we enable young people to experience work and different industries while still at school?
