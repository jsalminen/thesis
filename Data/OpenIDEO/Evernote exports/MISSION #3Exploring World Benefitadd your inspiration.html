MISSION #3Exploring World Benefit
add your inspiration
Let’s explore what ‘world benefit’ means to each of us and how we identify the actions and behavior that help us achieve it.

What does world benefit mean to you? What does it look like? And how do we know when we’ve gotten there? There’s no one-size-fits-all definition, but if we’re trying to identify the companies that are working to achieve world benefit – and inspire other companies to join in – it’s important to know what we’re working towards. What do you think of when you hear the term ‘world benefit’? What stories, photos, videos or other visuals can you share to show what world benefit means to you?

In particular, let’s think about what world benefit might look like in a business context. As examples:

  • developing innovative business solutions to address global problems like clean water, energy, education and to doing less harm (eg: companies that reduce their carbon footprint or commit to fair trade)
  • creating and marketing green products
  • rethinking how business resources are allocated to deliver the same or better value to customers
  • mobilising people to connect locally around issues which matter to them
  • meeting the needs of the world’s low-income communities (eg: designing products or services that directly benefit  the base of the pyramid)
