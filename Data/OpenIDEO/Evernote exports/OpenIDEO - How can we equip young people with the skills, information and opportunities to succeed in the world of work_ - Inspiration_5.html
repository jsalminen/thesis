Showcasing, Marketing & Storytelling

Existing professional sites focus on academic and work experience, which means that those without employment history are only able to celebrate their academic accolades. This can exclude those without a strong academic pedigree from standing out to employers – not to mention that academic track record offers only one indication of a young person's employability or potential.

Let's create concepts that enable young people to celebrate their skills beyond academic successes. A good story deserves to be well told.

How might we better enable young people to show all their abilities in one place?
How might we enable young people to showcase their strengths online?
