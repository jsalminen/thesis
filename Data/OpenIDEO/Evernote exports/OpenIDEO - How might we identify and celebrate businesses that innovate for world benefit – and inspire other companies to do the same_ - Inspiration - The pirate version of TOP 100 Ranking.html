Mission #2 Recognising the Best Find out more...

The pirate version of TOP 100 Ranking

The TOP 100 of the most unsustainable businesses of the year.
The Razzie Award
Businesses, communities, governments and organizations will hate this public ranking (for image reason etc.) and consumers could use it as recommendation/ guideline for their consumption or criticism. 

Curated by organizations like Greenpeace or similar. Ranking categories could be e.g. social, environment, local, global etc.. Here, the user/ consumer could curate too; by publishing their experienced story with unsustainable businesses.

Thereby these businesses will be inspired to improve; hopefully.

Additionally: A Golden Raspberry-Award-like honor for the "winner" could be even more effective /and fun.
