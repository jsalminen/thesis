Mission #2 Host an Interview
Let’s collect insights from people we know to learn more about e-waste. We've listed three options for interviews you could conduct. Know someone who fits into one or more of these categories? Go for it! Think of someone else who'd be interesting to speak to? We'd love to hear from them too.

Option 1:
Visit your local recycling center, electronic retailer, or sustainability expert. Find out what you can about where our consumer electronics really go after they enter the trash or the recycling bin. What tips, lessons or guidance could they provide for people who want to safely and properly dispose of tech tools?

Option 2:
Do you know someone that has to always have the latest gadget? Spend time with them to understand why they buy, what they buy and what they do with their outdated items. What is their expectation of what happens to their gadgets once they enter the trash or recycling bin?

Option 3:
Do you know someone who tries to live and shop ‘green’? Interview them to find out about how they shop, what choices they make and how they inform themselves about their product selections.
