Building Optimism & Motivation

Growing the potential of future generations means helping young people develop a sense of optimism and motivation about themselves and their lives. In Inspiration we were reminded that the first steps in solving youth employability should foster these traits at an early age, through the introduction of role models or other methods.

With this theme, we seek new ideas, services, initiatives, products that might help to grow the optimism and motivation in young people.

How might we help young people feel a sense of achievement?
How might we enable young people to embrace and learn from failure?
How might we teach young people qualities and soft skills such as creative confidence?
