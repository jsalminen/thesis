There's been a big buzz at OpenIDEO this week as we launch our brand new challenge which asks how might we improve maternal health with mobile technologies for low-income countries? We've partnered with Nokia and Oxfam on this challenge and it's already drawing wide attention from the likes of Fast Company – plus got featured on the Twitter home page during our launch! And here's more encouragement from Oxfam to harness your creativity around a life-changing issue. Global inspirations are pouring in on products, services, campaigns, maternal health experiences and mobile phone innovations. If you're keen to promote the challenge at your office, college, community group, etc – feel free to download the challenge poster.

The Compostmodern conference was a huge hit in San Francisco over the weekend. Providing a fertile ground for designing a sustainable future, OpenIDEO was a featured guest – adding to the notion that we all have a place in this conversation.

We're hoping to catch you online this week. Feel free to come over to our Support Forums and suggest features, ask questions and tell us your OpenIDEO-related news. We look forward to your ever-inspiring input!
