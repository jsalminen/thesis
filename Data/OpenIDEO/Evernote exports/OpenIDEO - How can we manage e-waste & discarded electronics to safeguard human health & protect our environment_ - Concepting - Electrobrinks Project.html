Electrobrinks Project

This concept was generated in the AIESEC brainstorm last month. We were invited to take the e-waste challenge to a group of graduate students from AIESEC (http://www.aiesec.org.br/). There were almost 80 students bringing ideas to the challenge. We used the Brainstorm in a Box toolkit adapted. It was very exciting and we proudly share one of the concepts generated here.

This concept was generated in the AIESEC brainstorm from the challenge:

How can we educatechildren and create awareness about the inherent problems of disposal of electronic waste?

Event's program:

Where it happens 
In public and private schools.

Who participates in the project
Volunteers (youth and students, trainees and AIESEC members) and all stakeholders.

Inside the project:

-First day: dynamic activity with painting, stories and explanation of the project (cartoon, comic strips), prompting the children to bring e-waste.

-Second day: workshops for the assembly of new toys from the junk they brought.

-Thirdy day: develop a story that will serve as a roadmap for a puppet play with toys made​​by children with the theme "Re-use of electronic devices"

-Fourth day: the final contest of the project with activities and games focused on the theme.
