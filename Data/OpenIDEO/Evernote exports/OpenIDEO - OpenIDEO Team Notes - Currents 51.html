After avid inspiration, canny concepting and enthusiastic evaluation, the Vibrant Cities Challenge winners have been announced! Congrats to *everyone* who participated – whether you posted inspirations, concepts, comments or evaluations – the winning ideas, as always, reflect the collaborative approach and vitality of our community. If you plan on taking any of the concepts forward in your local communities by way of prototypes, drop us a line at hello@openideo.com as we'd be keen to help you share developments via our upcoming Realisation phase for the challenge.


This month's Featured OpenIDEATOR is Jason George from Chicago who was a winner on the recent Amnesty Challenge. His take on OpenIDEO: "it’s like being in the middle of the biggest design-jam-session on the planet." Keep an eye out for more insight on our diverse global community members coming up each month.
