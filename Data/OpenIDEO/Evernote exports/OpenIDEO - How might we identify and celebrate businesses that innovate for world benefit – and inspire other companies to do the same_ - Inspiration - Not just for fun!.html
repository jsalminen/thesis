Mission #2 Recognising the Best Find out more...

Not just for fun!

Bored? visit 9gag - Just for fun. Want to learn about businesses and their efforts to innovate for world benefit? where to go?
9gag - Just for Fun

Social media marketing is one of the most powerful channels to advertise companies today. This is mind, to have a central repository of all companies and their stories on how they create sustainable social, environmental, and financial value for their stakeholders and communities would prove to be beneficial for these businesses. This would provide a venue where everyone can read through the stories, comment, vote, and be inspired with the best practices that these businesses are doing and the results they have achieved so far.

Inspired by the success of 9gag, the site should be simple enough for all readers to understand and be interested in. Information on the processes and results of the businesses should be available and everyone can vote. This would determine which businesses should be highlighted and recognized in the site.
