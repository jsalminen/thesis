Work&Teach - connecting the worlds of work and education, to the benefit of both

Work&Teach pays high-flying employees to deliver inspiring local after-school clubs. It is a development programme for participants, gives teachers new expert resources, and gives learners real-life role-models.
 
Young leaners




Work&Teach is looking for host schools and participants for pilots now.
 
 

How will your concept support young people as they transition into the world of work?

Work&Teach allows young people to interact with older role models that have actually left school, helping to contextualise their learning and bring the latest practices and ideas to life.
 

What online or in-person components of your concept will best support this transition?

There is an initial online brokering platform to match the needs of schools with the skills and backgrounds of participants. e.g. if a school has low maths attainment and poor attendance, Work&Teach could place individuals in the school who have a military background and a degree in computer science.
