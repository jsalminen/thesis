How can we change this picture? Join in on our Vibrant Cities Challenge to help us!


Bursting with excitement at OpenIDEO this week! Together with Steelcase, we've just launched our Vibrant Cities Challenge which asks How might we restore vibrancy in cities and regions facing economic decline? The Inspiration phase has got off to a roaring start – so come join us to gather existing insights on this lively topic. Watch the intro video to find out why revitalisation means so much to Steelcase. And be sure to check out our Lowdown on Inspiration if you're keen to get the scoop on this collaborative phase which feeds upcoming ideas.


Meanwhile, the Amnesty Challenge is about to switch to the Refinement phase. This is where we create a shortlist and ask folks to fine-tune those ideas – based on their own thoughts and fueled by comments from our community. Let's all join forces and figure out how to increase impact on these stellar ideas to support human rights!
