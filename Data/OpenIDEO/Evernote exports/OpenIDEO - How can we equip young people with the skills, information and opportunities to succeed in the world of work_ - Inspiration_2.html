Career Planning & Discovery

In the Inspiration phase we were struck by how few of us had ever been given any meaningful career advice – and in the cases that we had, we questioned whether it had set us on the right path. Given that so few young people understand different careers early on, and yet we're expected to make decisions that might close doors to our ideal careers, it's clear that the OpenIDEO community passionately believes in the power of planning.

With this theme we seek new concepts to help those without any employment history explore careers open to them and plan accordingly.

How might we help the very young think through career options so that they make smarter decisions early?
How might we help young people create a roadmap of actions in and out of education toward their dream career?
