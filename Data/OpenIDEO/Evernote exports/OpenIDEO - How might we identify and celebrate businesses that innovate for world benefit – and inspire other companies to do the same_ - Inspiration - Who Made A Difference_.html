Who Made A Difference?

Who Made a Difference is a world book of record aims to recognize and celebrate the action made by those companies that made net improvements to business, people and nature.
Steve Jobs at the 82nd Annual Academy Awards at the Kodak Theatre, Hollywood. March 7, 2010 Los Angeles, CA Pic...
 

This idea was inspired by those people who influenced and changed the world. One of those is Steve Jobs, best known as the co-founder, chairman, and chief executive officer of Apple Inc. Through Apple, he was widely recognized as a charismatic pioneer of the personal computer revolution and for his influential career in the computer and consumer electronics fields; Nelson Mandela who campaigned for justice and freedom in his South Africa; Albert Einstein who developed theories of relativity that has a very significant scientific breakthrough. Their contributions may be less powerful and influential but they really made a difference to improving the world. We can do the same way for those organizations that made the most of change and transform themselves to innovative social entrepreneurs. Who Made a Difference is a world book of record aims to recognize and celebrate the action made by those companies that made net improvements to business, people and nature.
