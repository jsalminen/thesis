Recycling model in the mind while develping every new product.

Oblige e-companies to be envoirmentally responsible and to develop a necessary recycling model in the devices LCA while developig every new product. Support development of international cooperation on step-by step recycing process.
epson product LCA
We will kill our planet with waste if will not have in mind its reuse and recycling in the process of developing every new product.

Goverments and international endovment fundations should support step by step developing of recycling products

Best products should be made in easy to recycle process.

How does your concept safeguard human health and protect our environment?

1 It will save planets raw resourses

2 It will create a green jobs worldwide

3 It will PREVENT E-WASTE in the first place and will change concept from WASTE to precious reusable materials

Where does your concept fit into the lifecycle of electronic devices?

It will inspire companies and goverments to use systemic envoirmentally friendly and economically beneficial approach

Govermant and internalional organizations should develop laws and regulations to support that process

Ideally that process should include - Goverment (laws and regulations) - NGOs & Education on envoirmental responsibility - Envoirmentally responsible companies - Responsible Disposal models - developed manufactures in cooperation wth e-companies on e-waste step by step recycling and extracting materials - creating new products from recycled materials

What steps could be taken today to start implementing your concept?

1To make a law regulations on invoirmentally responsible producing and recycle
2 To change a concept from Waste to Precious Reusable Materials
3To develop educational and envoirmental public awareness campains with will help to develop a good values and sustainable habbits of consumers
4To oblige and to support all manufacturers to develop new products in easy to recycle way
5 Support developing of full chain of buisneses with will be responsible for every step of recycle products and extract materials
6Globally to change paradigm of buissness values to green circular economy benefits and envoirmental responsibility

What kinds of resources will be needed to fully implement and scale your concept?

1 Systemic aproach and strategy on all scales globally and locally
2 International and govermental law regulations
3 Developing of new E-waste taxes and recycling economic benefits
4 Cooperating with NGOs on public awarness campains
5 Educational specialists developing program with will help to develop green values and green habbits in schools
6 International endovment organizations will supprt developing of new green buisneses with their grant programs
7 UNDP in cooperation with goverments, helping them to develop effective strategies and green circular economy
