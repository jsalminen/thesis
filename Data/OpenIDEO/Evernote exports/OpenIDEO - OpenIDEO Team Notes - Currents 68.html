Inspiration from IDEO's Design Thinking for Educators 



It's inspiration abound on OpenIDEO this week! The Youth Employment Challenge is in full swing with folks posting a diverse range of existing examples which help equip young people to succeed in the world of work. Join in to share and discuss across our range of Missions: Skills & Training, Hurdles & Success Stories, Collaborative Partnerships, The Employer's Perspective, and Surprise Us. And if you're new to OpenIDEO, check out our Lowdown on Inspiration to kickstart your collaborative input.


To help gather human-centered insights, our IDEO London office set up a video booth to capture responses on youth employment issues. This in turn motivated OpenIDEATOR Arjan Tupan to post his own Story of an Internship. Do you have a story to share? Why not have a go at making a video and posting it to inspire others and spark discussion?


And as if there wasn't enough excitement already – we're launching a new challenge next week! Stay tuned for a fresh opportunity to collaborate for social good.
