Aug 9th 2010 1 Week of Learning...

 

In the spirit of openness we thought that it would be cool to share some of our early site statistics – they make us more convinced than ever that OpenIDEO could really make a difference.

Over the course of the last week OpenIDEO received:



33k visits

23k absolute unique visitors

126k pageviews

And our favorite stat: visits from 121 countries

Here’s an image from Google Analytics showing those countries:



The contributions to the site have been great and so have the suggestions for improvements.  Thank you all for your patience as we’ve tried to rectify the issues.  To make that process more transparent we’ve created a bug-list here - please do add new ones that you spot.

Thanks again,



 The OpenIDEO team - Tom, Haiyan & Nathan 



Please email general thoughts to: hello@openideo.com
