Live Event Alert! Grameen Webcast



Keen to know more about Caldas and social business for our Grameen Social Business Challenge? Here's your chance! This Tuesday, June 28, from 8am - 9am PST – our friends at the Grameen Creative Lab in Colombia will be hosting a live webcast especially for the challenge and to celebrate Worldwide Social Business Day. It'll be packed with challenge-related goodness: outlining themes which have emerged from the Inspiration phase, launching the imminent Concepting phase and taking your questions on social business and life in Caldas – to help us dig deeper towards devising awesome Concepts to create impact to improve health for low-income communities.

You can participate live on the Grameen Worldwide Social Business Day 2011 page, where the live webcast will be embedded at the timing given above. And get your questions on Caldas and social business ready – you'll be able to ask those via a live-chat feature. You might want to check out images from Caldas which the Grameen Creative Lab has been posting already to get you thinking about what life is like on the ground there. For those of you who aren't able to join us – we'll have the presentation up on the OpenIDEO site soon afterwards.

We're looking forward to having you join us with your enthusiasm and creative curiosity for this great opportunity to gain challenge-related insights as we kick-off the ever-spirited Concepting phase!
