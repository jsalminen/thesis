iexpress

The concept is about being able to record and having your track record of what you like in front of you.It could be what one really likes to do and have a format to understand one's versatile interests which can work as a CV for later.
logo
"iexpress" is a web application through which young people can express what they want to do, what they like or places they love to visit or articles they like to read and it will keep generating a statistical report of what their interest lies in the most.Â 
iexpress as a process can start at a personal level and then grow into a community level.
how to express?
click an image what you have done. Be visual about it and upload to this diary where it will record under tags.
what to express?
It can be something that you have read, some place that you have visited, something that you were told, some gossip, some rumour, something that you made, something you hate, someone you want to meet.
when to express?
Try to keep it everyday. Essentially as regularly as possible.
who can express?
anyone can. The earlier you start using this, the better and more clear you are about what you want/what you like.


How will your concept support young people as they transition into the world of work?

Young people need someone else to second what they are thinking. The concept will help young people feel confident of their interests from a very early age.
It will help them dig deeper, because they know their interests.
A visual representation of how they have transitioned will help them gain clarity about themselves.

What online or in-person components of your concept will best support this transition?

A web application needs to be set up for this that can work to receive inputs from any like of interest. Social networking sites would enable a person to connect to this more easily and substantiate the virtual image of a person.
